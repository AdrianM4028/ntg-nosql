objectives of promoting a socially conscious and responsible
theatre movement, producing those drama which irrespectives

such socially responsible dramas among the common people
in metropolitan such theatre to reach a wider audience so that
Hindi theatre can become mass based.

Theatre Workers. In these 16 years of intense dedicated
activities we have produced 15 successful productions including one Street Play and staged our plays almost all over
