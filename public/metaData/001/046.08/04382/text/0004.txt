ABOUT THE NOVELIST
Laxman Maruti Gaikwad was born on July 23, 1956 in Dhanegaon, Latur

District, Maharashtra. Gaikwad gained international recognition with the 1998

publication of The Branded, a translation of his autobiographical novel, Uchalya.
Considered to be a masterpiece, his novel brings to the world ofliterature the trials .
and tribulation of his tribe for the ﬁrst time. The word 'uchalya’ literally meaning
the pilferers, was coined by the British, who classiﬁed the tribe as a criminal tribe.
His treatment of the Dalit theme ofwhich his own delicate subjectivity is a part is
widely acclaimed for its masterful sensitivity and supreme craftsmanship. He
depicts in all their subtlety and poignancy the inner feelings, sufferings and
emotional complexities ofa tribe historically viewed as criminal.
He has been the President of Jankalayan Vikas Sanstha since 1986, and of Denotiﬁed and
Nomadic Tribe Organization since 1 990. He has received many prestigious awards like the Mukadam
Award, Samta Award and the SahityaAkademi Award in l 988.
He has four books to his credit. His work Uc/zalya has been translated into many languages.
