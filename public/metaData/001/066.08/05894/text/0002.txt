Founded on 19 July 1981 , Natya Shodh Sansthan, the only institution of its
.kind in the country, has collected and studied over the fourteen years of its
existence a substantial amount of archival material related to theatre in India in
all its manifestations — classical and contemporary, urban and folk and tribal,

traditional and experimental, entertainment and educative.
