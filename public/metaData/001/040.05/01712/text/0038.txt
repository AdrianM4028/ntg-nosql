THE REPERTORY COMPANY

The Repertory Company of
the National School of Drama is a

performing group of players and
technicians who have graduated
from the School in the past years.
Formed in 1964 with just four
actors, the Company was started
on an experimental basis by Shri
E.

Alkazi,

the

Director

of

the

School till the late 1970's and a
dynamic theatre personality who
had a major hand in nurturing and
expanding the Company over the
years.
In 1977, when the Company
acquired the status of National
Repertory, Shri Manohar Singh
took over as its Chief and carried
its

activities

across

the

nation,
