Prashant Gautam Gupta
Prashant Gautarn graduated from Govt. College of Arts, Kolkata in 1986.
From that point on he has had a diverse and successful artistic career that has
spanned various media, subjects and countries.

As a Visual artist he has participated in many solo and group exhibitions in
Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata and abroad. In addition to painting he has explored
the medium of sculpture, installation, mask making, puppet making,
photography and textile designing.

For the past decade he has been a visiting faculty at National School of
Drama. In addition to teaching he has also worked with eminent theatre
directors on set, properties and costume designs.
