LIGHT DESIGN

गौतम मजुमदार

Gautam Majumdar
After having completed his graduation in Dramatic Arts from Rabindra
Bharti University in Acting in 1992, he completed his postgraduate
Diploma in Design and Direction from the National School of Drama in
1997.
He has worked as a Mime and Movement instructor at the Jogesh Mime
Academy from 1993 to 1994. He is presently a regularjudge ofMime at the
All India University Association. He has co-ordinated various theatre

workshops in the country and has collaborated with innumerable Indian
and foreign directors in the capacity of a designer, for which he has
travelled extensively to countries like Finland, Moscow, Denmark,
Germany. England, Japan, South Africa and Sri Lanka. His areas of
specialization in design are Lighting Design and Set Property Design and
their execution. At present he is a visiting faculty at the National School of
Drama.
