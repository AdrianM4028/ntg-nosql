THE COMPANY

The Chorus repertory theatre, Imphal was established in the
valley of the Small hill State, Manipur in the North-Eastern
India in April, 1976.
This repertory has been able to bring out many outstanding
productions of both original and translated plays. Perhaps, the
only well known theatre repertory in the North— Eastern Zone
of India established as a professional Company, this repertory
has been set up as an institution also to give training to

young artistes in the ﬁeld of direction, acting, stagecraft etc.
and to encourage theatre workers to experiment in original
Indian traditional styles in juxtapositions with the modern
techniques to give totally a new dimension to contemporary
Indian theatre movements aiming towards International arena

of theatre and its understanding.
