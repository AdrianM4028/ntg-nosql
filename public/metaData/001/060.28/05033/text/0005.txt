We can bestow a beautiful world to our future generation, only if we learn to cherish

it ourselves. We can put an end to war and its aftermath only if we develop more
humane feelings towards others. ’KAAFlLA’ is an effort in this direction to create
awareness against hatred, anger and gruesome behavior.

jungles of Assam where they come across an in ﬁl trator and take him captive. Here we can

against the cruelties meted out to the prisoners of war, which was also a topic of
discussion during the Geneva convention. .
