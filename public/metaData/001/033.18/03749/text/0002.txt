about the play
“Banailey” is a play apposite to the
contemporary times, a time when consumerism
has taken over and genuine human ethos have

taken a beating. In this play Man’s perpetual

greed has been contrasted against his anabolic
tendencies for harmony and wisdom.
about the director

Suman Kumar,a graduate from National
School of Drama, passed the Certiﬁcate Course in
Design from Mountview Theatre School, London.
He was awarded Charles Wallace India Trust
Scholarship in 1998. He has also participated in
the International Theatre Festival at Copenhagen.
He has been associated with ‘Bahrdop’ as an NSD
expert under extension teaching programme.
about the group

Bahroop came into existence in 1996,
when a group of people from different walks of
life decided to share its experience with a larger
section of the society. Its productions Kale Kauwe
Ne Kaha, Dekh Tamasha Dekh and Baaghicha

Bachharam Ka were awarded by Delhi Urdu
Academy in 1997, 1999 and 2000 respectively.
Other successful productions are Kath Jogi,
Madhvi, Tootte Parivesh and Chana’ Zamin Ka
Tuk'ra A ur Mein. Bahroop has also organized
talks, seminars and workshops with eminent
personalities like Habib Tanvir, J N Kaushal,

Vinit Kumar Sinha, Mushtak Kak, Chittaranjan
Tripathi, Shrikant Kishore, Suman Kumar, Piyush
Mishra, Kama] Kishore, Arvind Gaur, JD

Ashwalhama, Suresh Bharadwaj, Satyabrat Raul
and Surendra Sharma. Bahroop also publishes a
theatre magazine, ‘Bahroop Theatre Digest’.
