GILLES C H U YA N
Trained in France in Folk Dance, Modern Jazz, Ballet and Contemporary, he has also

been exposed to different kinds of ball room: Jive, Waltz, Tango and Salsa. Based in
India for the last 11 years, he studied Chhau Mayurbhanj under the guidance of
Shashidharan Nair from Shriram Bharatiya Kala Kendra.
Involved in numerous programmes all over India and abroad, he has been performing as
a dancer and actor and choreographing shows in different backgrounds and various

vocabularies: Dance shows in ball room, contemporary, Fusion and Classical forms,
Theatre, Fashion, Television, Cinema and concept performances for the corporate
world .
