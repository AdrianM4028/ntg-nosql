Light Designer

PA RAG SA R M A H
Paraj Sarmah graudated from the National School of Drama in the year 1995, with
specialization in acting. Later he joined NSD Repertory Company and completed his
six year tenure as an artist. He has acted under various eminent directors like Ratan

Thyam, Ram Gopal Bajaj, D.R. Ankur, Satyadev Dubey, Pitro Ceslak, and MK.
Raina. Some of the well—known plays he has acted in like Dilli Chalo, Mitti Ki Garhi,

Rani Sethani, Bhookh Aag Hai and Ghasirama Kotwal.
Apart from acting, direction, set 86 light design have always interest him. The play
Parashuram directed by him has won national acclaim and has been staged at the

Nandikar Theatre Festival 1997—98 and at the Bharat Rang Mahotsav in 2001. He is
known for his light design especially in plays such as Raaste, Bhand Duhai, I’arinati,
Tajmahal Ka Tender, Bandi Pramath, Mukta Dhara and also for Shatrupa a sound and
light production by the Song and Drama Division.
