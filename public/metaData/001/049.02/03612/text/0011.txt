K R I T l V. S H A R M A
Has designed costumes for eminent directors like Ebrahim Alkazi, Amal Allana,

Anuradha Kapur, M.K. Raina, Ram Gopal Bajaj, Valentine Teplyakov, Robin Das,
Bhanu Bharti, John Russel Brown, Late. B.M. Shah, Rita Ganguli, Alok Chatterjee,
Wolfgang Kolenider and Fritz Benewitz.

Her Associations with the National School of Drama and its repertory company and
Shri Ram Center for Art and Culture have been for more than ten years. She has also
designed for films and television and has been a segment director for Surabhi (a

telemagzine). Presently she is working as a teacher of Drama and Theatre Studies at the
British School, New Delhi, where she has directed several plays like Yerma, The Trojan

Women and The Crucible.
