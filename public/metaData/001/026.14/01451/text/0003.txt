Jana Natya Manch has been doing theatre in Delhi and northern India since 1973. All along, the

concern to take theatre to the people has informed our work. Till 1978 we did proscenium plays,
but did not restrict them to the auditoria—they were performed also in the open, for audiences
of thousands. In 1978, we started street theatre and have written and performed 40 street plays
so far. Since 1988, we have also started doing proscenium theatre. The annual Safdar Hashmi
Memorial Lecture series and the quarterly Ianam Nukkad Samvad have been started since 1993,
