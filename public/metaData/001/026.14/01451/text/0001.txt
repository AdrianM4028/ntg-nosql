9, 10, 11, 12 मार्च 1995, शाम 6-33 बजे, श्रीराम सेंटर सभागार

A world, where on moonlit nights, lovers snuggle in the mango- grove
and dream, unaware of the fish-net of life waiting to entangle them.

Poverty-stricken parents watch their daughters cross their threshold
and walk into a bleak marital future.
Nature’s fury threatens to drown and dismember primordial bonds
between the tiller and the earth. . .
. . . And nowhere do the bells ring.
Somewhere, within the folds of this shrouded existence, lies a ques-

tion, waiting to be raised. And when a brave voice raises it, hands clasp,
hearts swell, voices rise, fists clench, eyes scream, shoulders join. . .
. . . And the bells ring with an earth-shattering echo.
