About the Play
Houdong Lomboiba Amasung Pebet
A wild cat disturbed the life of the little bird, Pebet with its chicks, who lived in a bush very

peacefully and happily. The cunning cat reached the bush where Pebet & its chicks lived by
coming from door to door. He started seeking the opportunity to catch the birds. The cunning cat
was looking for any chance to catch the young and tender birds day in and day out. The wise
mother of the birds knew the cunning nature of the cat and taught its chicks any sort of technique
to escape from the hands of the cat. Thus, as the time passed on, the young birds became almost
matured and strong expect one who was very weak physically. One day the cunning cat tried its
best to catch the birds but the birds ﬂew away. But the unlucky and weakest bird was unable to
escape from the cat and caught by him. The foolish cat followed what the mother bird told him.
Now the young bird after bath and basking in the sun had the capacity to ﬂy. Thus, when
throwing up in the sky the young bird ﬂew away towards its mother and brothers, casing itself on
the paw of the foolish cat. The cat licked it and said it was tasty. Then he murmured what a good
taste the bird’s ﬂesh would have.
