partition to raise voice against cornmunalism, and serialization of plays
on literacy on the: All India: Radio. He worked for three years with the

communal amity that has existed through ages. Presently he is exploring
the sufi Nazeer and sings his songs with his group whom he has trained.

He has directed several plays including “Thank you Mr. Glad" and
“Mareech Sarnvad” and acted in many others.
