Born in 1953, Mr. Ghosh started to learn Table
from Pandit Vishwanath Bose, at the age of 7.
He has also learnt Hindustani Vocal Music
from Acharya K.C.D. Brahaspati and
SmtSulochana Brahaspati.
Mr. Ghosh started composing music for plays
from 1971 and started as a professional
freelance music composer and music director
from 1974. He has composed more than two
hundred nationally popular plays for stage and
television which includes :

Kahaniyan, Tracking gold, Opening stroke,
Devi Choudhurani etc.

Mr. Ghosh has composed music for various TV
films, which include Fakhroor Mian, Lal ghas

per neela ghore, Kath Ki gaadi etc. For
documentary films, he has composed music for
In Secular India, Troches of courage, Journey
through Kashmir, Willow Warriors, etc.

In 1980 he was awarded for the best Musical
Play by Sahitya Kala Parishad for the play
Yahudi Ki Ladki. In 1982 he has been felicitated
by Poland State Television. In 1986-87 he got the
N .H.K. Award by Japanese Television. In 1989
he was awarded medals in North Korea. In
1991 he received the Kala Sahitya Evam
Sanskriti Puraskar by Pahal of Faridabad. In
2000 Mr. Ghosh was awarded Sangeet Natak
Academy Award for contribution to Theatre
Music.
