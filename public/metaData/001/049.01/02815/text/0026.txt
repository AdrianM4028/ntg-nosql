NATIONAL SCHOOL OF DRAMA REPERTORY COMPANY

National School of Drama Repertory was formed in 1964-65 on an experimental basis
by E.Alkazi. In the beginning, it consisted of only four actors. It later expanded into a
larger group of actors. musicians and technicians. mainly drawn from the graduates of
the National School of Drama, Delhi. In 1964, the late Om Shivpuri led the company
and later in 1977, Manohar Singh became the first regular Chief of Repertory. When
Manohar Singh left the Company in 1985, for a short period Ms.Uttara Baokar and Ms.
Surekha Sikri looked after the functioning of the company. Later J. N . Kaushal officiated
as the Chief till 1988. From 1988 to 1994 Prof. Ram Gopal Bajaj was heading the
company. From 1994 to 97 Kaushal functioned again as acting Chief.
