Director

After passing MA.

in Hindi Literature

from

University of Delhi, He joined National School of
Drama and received Diploma in Dramatic Arts with
specialization in Direction. He had a short stint as
Senior Drama Instructor in National Bal Bhavan,

New Delhi. Thereafter, he joined NSD Repertory

Company for two years.
His first assignment as a teacher of Dramatic
Literature, Theatre Architecture and Direction was

with Bhartendu Natya Academy, Lucknow as a
Visiting Teacher during 1978—79. Theareafter, he
joined NSD as Associate Professor (Classical Indian
Drama & Aesthetics) in 1979 and served in this

capacity for two years. Thereafter He taught modern
Indian drama, theatre architecture at NSD from 1988—

95.
In 1996, He was selected for the post of Professor

(Extension Programme). In this capacity he served for

seven years. It was during his term as Professor (Extension
Programme) that the Extension Programme achieved
dizzy heights and in collaboration with the local theater
groups, NSD have organized theatre workshops all over

India.

He has pioneered a totally new genre in modern Indian
Theatre called 'Kahani Ka Rangmanch' with the NSD
Repertory Company in 1975. He has presented many
stories and novels in the series of Kahani Ka Rangmanch.
He has also directed a good number of plays with the
students of NSD.
