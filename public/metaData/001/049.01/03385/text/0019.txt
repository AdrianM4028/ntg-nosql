THE DIRECTOR

Robin Das
Born in 1951 in a village in Orrisa, he studied science
and economics before entering the National School of
Drama, New Delhi, for professional training in theatre
with a specialization in design. Founder and artistic
director of FAMIT (Forum for Assimilation of Modern
lndian theatre), he is known for directing experimental
performance of diverse genre, each time coming up with
new assimilative approach to the art of direction and

creating designs for unusual performance space.
Currently Professor of Scenic Design at the National
School of Drama, a self taught sculptor and visual artist.
He is continuously seeking a multidisciplinary approach
to training in the plastic and performing arts while
assimilating the ancient and traditional with the
