BALLET ANTAR CHHAYA

Ballet AN’I‘AR CHI-IAYA is the reﬂection of the choreographer's memories of
pre- Independence and the present state. The ﬁrst part of the Ballet has the

images of Colonizers. the Bondage , Revolt, Law, Prisoners and the
Messenger of Peace —~ the Ghost within and the Hands of Violence, ending
as a question ” Where am I ?"
