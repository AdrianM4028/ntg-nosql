On her way back from her father's kingdome
Meghasuri started persuing Nanda. She rushed
from one place to another, destroying Lodhia
demon, Hatiya demon and Ranuwa demon. But
Meghasuri persisted in following her. Nanda was
tired. She took a hair out of her hairdo and threw it
on the grounds from which sprang a tiger. The tiger
punced upon Meghasuri and killed him.
There is a Nanda in each woman. If she realizes it,

she can destroy the likes of lechrous demons and
incestuous Meghasuris.
Devendra Ioshi
