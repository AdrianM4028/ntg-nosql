Members of Shriram Bharatiya Kala Kendra Trust, Governing Council,
Faculty all the staff and Artistes wish to place on record their deep sorrow on
the sudden passing away of Shri Mohan Upreti, who had a close association
with SBKK and whenever any help was required he willingly gave it. Shri

Mohan Upreti's contribution to the activities of the SBKK and to the arts of
the hill people of the country have been invaluable. He set up and nutured
the ‘ Parvatiya Kala Kendra', and brought the culture and arts, including music
and dance of the mountain ranges to the rest of the country through this
medium. No other worker in the ﬁeld has achieved so much excellence in
the quality of presentations. Mohanji was himself much a lively character
with a vivacity and intensity that will be sorely missed. His passing away
has left a gap which will be impossible to ﬁll.

The members of the Shriram Bharatiya Kala Kendra Trust, Governing
Council, faculty and all the artistes offer their heartfelt condolences to Smt.

Naima Upreti and-'other members of the bereaved family and pray to God to
give peace to the departed soul, and strength to Mrs. Upreti and other members
of family to bear this irreparable loss.
