graduate from Indore has been active in theatre from
1985. Hejoined NSD in 1993 and graduated in 1996,
specializing in Design and Direction.
His ventures as a director include Holi (1992)
and Romeo-Juliet ( l 9 96) with his group ‘ ‘ Drama
Factory", Indore. His productions as a writer-director
