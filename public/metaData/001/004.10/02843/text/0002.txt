About the Director
Shri Satish Anand is a freelance and well
known theatre person. He did his B.Sc. in 1962',
and since 1971 has been closely associated with
theatre. He has had the honour ofbeing the Chairman of the Bihar Sangeet Natak Akademi. He
has also been the Theatre Advisor to the NCZCC
Allahabad in its programme Committee. He is
best known for his intense work in the Bidesia
theatre form of Bihar. By incorporating the dance
forms of the Bidesia style and their folk stories,
he has evolved a new theatre form of Bedesia.
He has conducted a number of theatre workshops in Patna. He has also acted in a number
of tele-ﬁlms. He is best known for his direction of
plays for the National School of Drama, Bhartendu
Natya Akademi, the Darpan Group, Kanpur. he
has directed about 80 plays till now. His most
famous plays are ‘Marjeeva’, ‘Aadhe Adhure’,
‘Khamosh! Adalat Jari Hai’, ‘Pagla Ghora’, ‘Andha

Yug’, ‘Asarh Ka Ek Din’, "I‘ughlaq’, "I‘hahra Hua
Pani’, ‘Ballabhpur Ki Roop Katha’, ‘Juloos’ etc. He
has also directed plays in Bidesia and Nautanki
folk art styles as well as one play in Gujrati.
