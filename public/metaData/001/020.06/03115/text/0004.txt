These are Panchatantra tales recreated and
in Kathavachak style. The idle Lion King and his

courtiers live on food supplied by the clever
Jackal. The Jackal plays a trick to dethrone the

king and replaces an Ox in its place for more
profit. But that also turns out to be impossible.
So the crafty Jackal paints himself blue to
appear as an angel and ascends the throne. The
intelligent Rabbit exposes the Jackal. But....who
will become the KING ?
