MADHAB MALANCHI KOINYA dramatizes a
simple narrative from the old Myrnensingh
Geetika ot East Bengal, but not folk drama.
The play has attempted to confront and recast
the original form, character, and narrative of
the geetika, and presented it in terms of
modern theatre practice.
