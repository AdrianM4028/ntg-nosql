Based on a story by Ka-zi Nazrul Islam
Mamtazuddin Ahmed's play centres round Bindi
who murders her drunkard, adulterous husband
and goes to prison. She returns home after serving
full term, only to find people calling her RAKKHUSI
(demoness) instead of Bindi.
