Director: Sima Mukhopadhyay
Sima Mukhopadhyay a noted actress of group Theatre. She was
presented the Best Actress award for the production ‘Balidan'
produced by Pachim Banga Natya ACademi in the year 1990. Her
maidan venture ‘Bali’ was an one act play. Subsequently she wrote
two plays ‘Bikalpa' & ‘Bhanga Baned’ and which were stagged by
her group ‘Rangrup’ and in those plays she was in the leading role.
‘Je Jan Achhe Majhkhane' is the latest play written by her and directed
as well.
