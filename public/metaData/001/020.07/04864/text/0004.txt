'Asmita' stands committed to aesthetically innovative and socially relevant theatre. It takes
up contemporary issues to underline the contours of our time while providing the best of

entertainment. So far the group has staged Bhishma Sahni's "Hanoosh" (26 shows),
Albert Camus’ 'Caligula' (21 shows), Raghuvir Chaudhary's 'Sikandra Sani
(1 at Urdu Academy Festival-93), Dr. Girish Karnad's "Tughlaq' (17 shows), 'RaktKalyan' (37 shows), Dr. Dhararnvir Bharti's 'Andhayug' (9 shows), Brecht's

Exception & the Rule (street play) Shakespeare's 'Julius Caeser' (8 shows)
& Merchant of Venice (as 'Dilfarosh' at Urdu Academy's festival '96), Eugene
O'Neil's Desire under the Elms (14 shows), Swadesh Deepak’s Court-Martial
(52 shows), Narendra Mohan's Qalander (Urdu Academy's Festival 1997), Dario
Fo's Operation Three Star (26 shows) Mahesh Dattani‘s Final Solutions (23
shows) and Ashok Lal's Ek Mamooli Aadmi (30 Shows) Swadesh Deepak's
Sabse Udaas Kavita (15 shows) Shakespare’s Julius Caeser restaged in 1998
(2 shows). Brecht's Kanpur Ki Aurat Bhali Ramkali (15 Shows). Neil Simon's
Log Baag (10 shows) Nag Boda's Amma Tujhe Salaam (6 shows).
In support of people’s struggle against victimisation, “Asmita” gave street-shows of
Safdar Hashmi’s Aurat (36 shows) and Chicago 1886 (78 shows), Sanjha Jahan
(21 shows) and Tasveer (17 shows).
As an umbrella organisation to healthy theatre activities, “Asmita” organised workshops
at different colleges in Delhi which led to the production of Brecht's The Good Person
of Setzuan at I.P. College, Bhisham Sahni's Madhavi at Gargi College, Dario Fo's
Accidental Death of an Anarchist at School of Planning & Architecture and Arthur
Miller’s Crucible at LP. College. Brecht's the Caucasian Chalk Circle, and Neil
Simon's Log Baag at SPA. The group organised the two-and-half hour long solo
performance of Piyush Mishra which enthralled the audience at Jaipur and Calcutta also.
In recognition of its endeavours, the group has been invited to perform Court Martial as

the only entry in Hindi at National Drama Festival at Calicut (Kerala), 'Asmita' has
also performed 'Court Martial' at Dibrugarh (Chayanika), Assam and Jarnmu & Kashmir,
'Final Solutions' in Jabalpur and Gwalior 'Dperation Three Star' in Begu Sarai
(IPTA Golden Jubilee Festival), Shakespeare's "Julius Caesar' at Dibrugarh and
Sibsagar (Assam) for the National Shakespeare Festival organised by Chayanika,
scenes from Julius Caesar, Sabse Udaas Kavita and Rakt Kalyan at the Prithvi
International Theatre Festival (November 1998). Also performed Rakt Kalyan on

the occasion of Girish Karnad being bestowed the Kalidaas Samman and Gyanpeeth
Award. Also staged "Ek Mamooli Aadmi" for Bhartiya Rang Mahotsava (1999).
