Trishanku, Sheh-Ye-Maat and Yuddhaman. He has

also adapted the celebrated novels Mitro Marjani
and Animal Farm into Hindi, which were highly
appreciated. He has translated and adapted Theatre versions of many Indian and foreign dramas
as well. He has also contributed several articles on
Theatre in reputed Hindi and English journals and
periodicals.
