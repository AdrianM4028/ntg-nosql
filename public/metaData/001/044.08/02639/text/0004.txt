Some of the famous companies of that time are
Kavasjis Alfred Theatrical company, Baliwala’s
Victoria company.
By this time, the procenium, the wings, the
curtains, the drop - curtain had become the nec-

essary elements for Theatre. Every scene had a
different curtain, which would be very beautiful
and which would have pictures of palaces, gardens, jungle, heaven etc painted on it.
These settings and curtains would take a long
time to change. Thus “comic drama” was introduced so that as long as the setting was being
changed the audience would not get bored. Some

plays used the comic drama as an instrument to
criticize the viles of society.
The other elements in the play were poems,
songs, weighty dialogues, and beautiful dances.
The third element was amazing stage—effects.
The stage effects and songs were some of the
main reasons for the popularity of the Parsi Theatre.
