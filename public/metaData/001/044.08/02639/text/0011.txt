The play reflects two conceptual frame
works - One is the half-baked knowledge and
blind enthusiasm of the inexperienced youthThe other is of the irresponsible elders who
due to their own lusts for power, force the

youth to face grave dangers.
The play has been developed in such a
way that these statements become stronger.
Some of the irrelevant scenes, songs, stage
effects, comedy sequences have been deleted
so as to give a sharp edge to the action and
a tight tempo.
A lot of curtains and paraphernalia of sets
would obstruct the flow of action. So a minimum essential, suggestive set has been used
in its place. Yet to maintain the characteristics
of the Parsi style, the acting and the costumes

have been stressed upon.
One more thing : The Spakespearean style
rose and matured out of the Marlowe style of
theatre. It blossomed after doing away with
the faults and the defects in the Marlowe style
of play-writing. Similarly the Parsi Dramatic
style could easily have become the style of
todays Indian theatre, if only
the experienced, knowlegded
theatre persons, under the in—
fluence of certain notions had

not already rejected it because
of its melodrama.

Because, the Parsi style is
