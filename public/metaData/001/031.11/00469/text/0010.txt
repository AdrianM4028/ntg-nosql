C H A K KA C H A L DA H A

The Play
‘Chakka Chalda Hai' is a play of the soil. it is the story of a man alone, who
tried to resist modernisation that was fast treading for his little village. The stage is
set in the early 40s in a sleepy little village in Punjab; when change and industrialisation

were slowly setting in all over the country.
The lorry service had newly started and Gangaram’s village which had hitherto
been leading a quiet independent existence, suddenly became vulnerable. The lorry

syrnbolised modernisation and the evils that went along with it. Gangaram, the village
potter, foreseeing disaster for his family and the entire village could not oppose a whole
movement alone. But he tried ...........
