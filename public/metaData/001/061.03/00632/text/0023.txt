Translated, ‘Sohamasmi’ means ‘I Am 'I‘hat’.

This is a composition choreographed in the
Odissi style, which explored verses from the
‘ Ishopanishad (one of the principle Upanishads).
It symbolises the All-pervading Reality—the
underlying unity within the cosmos.
