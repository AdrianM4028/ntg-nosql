The Repertory Company of Kala Academy Goa, “Rangmell”
was established in 1992. It provides professional experience and

an exposure to the graduates of the School of Drama as well as the
local talent. It functions as a leading light by way of staging plays of

high standard in terms of their contents and presentation.
Rangmell has so far produced 14 plays in three different
languages with the same expertise.

It has performed 180 shows in Goa and the states of Maharashtra,
Madhya Pradesh, Gujarat, Rajasthan, Karnataka and Delhi. Special
efforts are being made to perform for the people in far flung village
and smaller towns where one rarely gets an opportunity to witness a
good theatrical performances. Recently Rangmell started weekend
theatre and the response is very encouraging. Rangmell takes pride

in presenting two of its productions ‘ Premjagor’ (in Konkani) &
‘Tiddorao’ (in Hindi) in this prestigious Theatre festival; The Bharat
RANG Mahotsav being organised by the N.S.D. HAPPY VIEWING.
The enthusiasm of organisers and appreciation of the audience
at all these places encourages us to go ahead with many more in
future.
