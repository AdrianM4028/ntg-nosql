COSTUME DESIGN
Amba Sanyal

In her early years, Amba Sanyal, essayed signiﬁcant roles in "Heer Ranjha" by Delhi Art Theatre and
as Kaikeyeein Ram Leela otharatiya Kala Kendra, for three years.

Initially she assisted Shanta Gandhi in costumes for Jasma Odan and subsequently went solo as a
costume designer for major NSD plays, like: "Birjees Qadar Ka Qunba", "Lower Depths", "Hamlet",
"Malavikagnimitram" "Poshak" and “Long day's journey into Night”. She has documented
Traditional Indian Textiles for the government of India and written two books on Sarees of India.
Presently she is teaching in Pearl Academy, a frontline institute of fashion and design.
