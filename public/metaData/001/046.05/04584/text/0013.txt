Subsequently, the play would have entered the
repertoire of Shakespeare’s company at the Globe
Theatre, where all of London society could enjoy it.
Shakespeare was a great plagiarist, and the sources
for the story of “A Midsummer Night’s Dream“ are
many and various. But, as ever. the strength and
originality of the work lies in the way that he makes
these stories his own through his dramaturgical skill ५
and above all through the rich and varied language
