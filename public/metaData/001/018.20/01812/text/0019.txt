Devadatta reaches the spot. He suspects Padmini's
intentions when he finds that she is missing from their
home.

She must have come for Kapil, he believes.

Kapil sees Devadatta; it is as though he has been
expecting him to arrive. For a brief moment, the strains
of the melody recall the old days of warmth and friendship. But they both love Padmini and all the three cannot
live together.
