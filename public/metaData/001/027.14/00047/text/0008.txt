The Delhi Childrens Theatre is a voluntary Organisation working in the field of
child development for the last 31 years. It was established in the year 1954 with the
blessings and patronage of the late Pt. Jawaharlal Nehru, the then Prime Minister of
India. This year we complete Thirty one years of performing in Art, Music, Dance Er
Dramafamong the Children in the age group of 5 to 15, particularly belonging to families
of modest income groups.

This year our Annual Festival is being held for 4 days from ३ 8th Feb. to 21st
Feb. at Mavalanker Auditorium. The young participants will put up three colourful
shows in the course of the day and present cultural and thematic items in the form of
dance, drama, ballet, mime and music.

The Delhi Children’s Theatre runs more than six Training Centres regularly during
the year in different schools of Delhi offering training to the young children under the
guidance of trained professional choreographers and music directors.

The production of the centres run by the Delhi Children's Theatre and also by
other institutional members of the Organisation are regularly being presented at their
annual festivals for the benefit of the young children and their parents. It is expected
that every day more than six thousand children will have the opportunity to witness our
grand shows presented by the children for children. The governing Body of the Delhi
Childrens Theatre will invite young boys and girls belonging to the depressed areas of the

city to witness their shows for which no gate fee will be collected from them.
Further, in order to commemorate the occasion, the Delhi Children's Theatre will
