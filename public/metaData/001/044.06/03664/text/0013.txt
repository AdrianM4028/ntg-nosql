Born on 2nd March, 1962 in Ujala Gopinath, Orissa in a family of agriculturalists, he has
completed his tenth grade and for the past fifteen years has been a perforner of "Pala" the leading
traditional narrative form of Orissa.
Having learnt "Pala" under guru Venudhar Sahu and Prashant Nath Sharrna "Dwari" under guru
Dusshasan Pradan, "Danda Naaoh" under guru Purna Chandra Pradhan, he has also worked
extentensively in ”Jatra" and " Ram Natak" (Rarnlila).
Besides working at the secretariat of Orissa for fifteen years he has also been awarded with
"Kala-Pathak". Ravindra Mandap Golabai Awards and honoured with the prestigious "Vadya
Yantra“title.
He has also worked with Prof. Robin Bee in " Rise and Fall of the City of Mahogonny" in 2003.
