Born in 4962 in Nayagadh, Orisa, Dusshasan Malik, did his schooling upto ninth standard. For the
last fifteen years he has been performing Palagayan, Danda Nach, Jatra all over Orissa under the
training and guidance of guru Prashant Nath Sharma. He has also worked with Prof. Robin Das in
"Rise and Fall of the City of Mahgony" in 2003.
