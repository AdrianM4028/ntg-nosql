A graduate from Utkal University in Economics, Robin Das graduated from NSD in 1976 and
joined it's teahcing faculty in 1977-78. Over the years he has worked on assirnilative and innovative
modes of designing, direction and approaches to acting. As a Designer he has fervently worked from
1976 to 1986, with most of the important Directors of the country. Producing exciting designs for
about fifty plays of diverse genre and as a Director brought forward many important aspects of
contemporary Indian Theatre Scenario. Apart from having directed about 55 plays, he has
sporadically worked as an Art Director and Actor for few films and media work. A self taught painter /
caricaturist, he continuously strives to renew teaching methodologies apart from aspiring to
assimilate the modern and the classical in play-presentation. He was honoured with Academy Award
2000 by Central Sangeet NatakAcademy for his contribution to Design in Theatre.
