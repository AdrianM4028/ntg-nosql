Born in 1957 in a peasant family in Jharpuda, Orissa, Bhaskar Parida undertook formal training
in Dhurki Natya from guru Bainsav Parida. He has been performing Dhurkinatya all over Orissa for
the last 17 years. He has performed at Nandikar, Calcutta also. He is the lead perforner of his own
reputed team of 6 people.

He has also worked with Prof. Robin Das in " Rise and Fall of the City of Mahogony" in 2003.
