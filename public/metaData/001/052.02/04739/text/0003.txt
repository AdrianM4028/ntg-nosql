About the Play /
The story revolves around a Blue Umbrella, which becomes an article of esteem
possession, signifying elated status of the person possessing it.

In the process of

acquiring that Blue Umbrella, the simple & beautiful expression of common day to day
human relations are expressed. And in the end the act of appreciating the other person’s
feelings and ﬁnally sharing with each o'ther beautifully sums up the joy of sharing and
giving rather than acquiring.
