About the Play

Suraj is a 13—14 year old boy. He is brilliant in many activities.
He is good in his studies, he
paints very well, he likes to make friends and is a darling of his mother,
father' and grandmother.
But he is troubled by the fact that he is not tall, broad and very strong.
He is obsessed with
having a physique like that. His best friend Rocky also wants to be
tall. Both search about for
new ways to be so. Suraj has two sisters. His younger sister Saloni is
upset her dark skin. She
feels she would have been happier had she been fair skinned. Everyone
would love her. These
three brothers and sisters have three other brother sisters as cousins. What
all happens when
these cousins get together for the Christmas holidays?,. Do Suraj and rocky have
a break up of
their friendship? What happens to their obsessive desires? How does Saloni
turn happier than
what she was earlier? Do all these children like Tiger or Not? Who this Tiger?
You come to
know of all this as the play progresses. The ‘devised’ play gives us the feel of the
immense and
intense intricacies of growing children-the very many complexes within them, which
are present
for valid reasons of their own: the. umpteen moments of failures and happiness;
the multiple
attempts by these children to overcome all these hurdles and We also get to sense the
interesting
triumph against “the odds” by children.
