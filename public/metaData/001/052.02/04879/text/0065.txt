A child is a creature that attracts all the
projected tropes and fears of the adults around
him. He is to be feared as well as loved, for he
represents both change as well as salvation for
the world. Some people can relate to a child,
some cannot; but no one can claim to
understand him fully. The child is by definition
a potential - a potential for anything. Expose
him to love and he will love; expose him to
hate and he will hate; expose him to the
wonderful and he will learn to wonder. Hence,
what he is to be exposed to has to be carefully
though out since it is from here that will learn
whatever he has to. Life is a quest and this
quest may turn out to be an answer to a puzzle.

In today‘s world. children have moved away
from nature and left behind their curiosity, if
this play rekindles these natural instincts in
chi ldren, I shall consider my work to be

successful.
