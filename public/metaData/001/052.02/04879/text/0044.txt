About the Director

Gourab Kumar Hazarika is a well—known
dance director of Assam. specializing in the
field of folk and traditional creative dance. He
has worked for various Assamese films. video
albums. the Assam Mobile Theatre Group and
Yatra Partha. a forum of folk theatre. Besides
Assam, Gourab has performed in Shimla.
Cuttack, Gurgaon, Delhi and Haryana. He has
received the Best Dancer Award and also the
Best Director of a Dance Drama Award in

national competitions. At present he is working
on folk and traditional forms with Shilpa Kanon
and holds regular workshops and stage shows
for/with children
