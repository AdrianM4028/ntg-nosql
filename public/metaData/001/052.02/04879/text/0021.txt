About the Director
Susanta Mandal has been involved in the field
of theatre for over a decade. His comprehensive
training in the performing arts includes training
in mime and movement. the Chhau dance.
martial arts. classical vocal music and shadow
puppetry. Over the years he has directed a
number of children‘s plays in Nandikar‘s Theatre—

in—Education project. including Badal Sarkar‘s
BEEG. a silent drama for deaf children. He is also
an actor and light designer as well as trainer for
many Nandikar programmes. Apart from having
received scholarships and fellowships he has
participated in several workshops, seminars, and
festivals in India and in Oman (2008).
