As a dance director and choreographer used to
directing dance—dramas, it was quite a
challenge for me to direct this play with and for
children. However, since the theme of the play
has to do with music, song and dance, I was
able to relate to them and achieve the end I had
in mind. It is always nice to work with children
who are flowing with so much energy and
creativity. I have simply tried to give this
creativity shape. Also. I have designed the play
according to the colourful and vibrant frames
that exist in the minds and eyes of children.
