Pre/n/i' Ho Natak was evolved in a theatre
workshop for children organized at Phalodi.
When I came in contact with the children there
i recognized their potential for expressing
themselves and their willingness to participate
in the initiative. My initial efforts were focused
on creating an environment of harmony and
mutual understanding for the children so that
they could develop vocal as well as physical
expressions and participate in the
development of a non—guided theme. The
play that emerged was, therefore, the result of
their own observations and comments about
their lives, social contexts and realities.
