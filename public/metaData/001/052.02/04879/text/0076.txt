The story of Choon-Hyang and her true love,
Mong Ryong. has been orally passed down
over several generations in Korea. Despite
loving each other immensely and proclaiming
their love, the two lovers are separated when
Mong—Ryong is forced to follow his father to
another city. Left alone. Choon Hyang must
now thwart the advances of a cruel Lord who
desires her submission and resist the
pressures of her mother. who also wants her to
abandon her love and give into the lord. The
play follows the events that leadto the union
oftthe two lovers. Packed witth energy and
showing how passion can change the world. it
is a heart—warming tale of love and resilience
for adults and children alike.
