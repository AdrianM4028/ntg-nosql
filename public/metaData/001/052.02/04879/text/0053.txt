About the llil'ector
Dr. Y. Sadananda Singh is one of the leading
mime artistes and directors in the country. A
recipient of several fellowships and
scholarships he has participated in many
festivals in both India and abroad. He has also
attended workshops by several experts (many
of them SNA awardees) at the national and
international levels and has directed numerous
short—plays, skits and mime plays.

Directorial

India has a rich artistic tradition and it is our
constant endeavor to bring about a fusion of
mime with the other indigenous art forms of
Manipur to create mime performances of
international standards Through our plays we
attempt to provide meaningful and entertaining
performances while also passing on certain
values to all who watch us.
