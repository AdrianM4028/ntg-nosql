From the beginning of time. human society has
been divided into the haves and the have~nots
the rich and the poor. Yet. the desire to do
something and fulfill one‘s dreams is not
limited to any one class or section of society.
And that is the theme of the play Ghat. The play
looks at the dichotomy that exists between
down—trodden children who are immensely
creative and have their own dreams and
aspirations on the one hand. and children from
a wealthy background who even though less
talented. never have to face the trails and
tribulations faced by the former. Yet. as the
play tries to show. one can surmount the
difficulties and challenges one is faced with
through a positive approach and committed
perseverance.
