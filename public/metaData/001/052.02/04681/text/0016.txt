Gotipua Dance : The Gotipuas are young boys who
dress as females and Dance. Along with the decline of
Devadasi or Mahari tradition, the class of these boy
dancers came in to existence who could carry out the
tradition and it is Largely from them that the Present
form of Odissi dance has been received. The dance
of the Gotipuas is in Odissi style but their technique,
costume and above all presentation differ from most
of the Maharis. The Singing is done by the dancers
themselves. Most of the present day Gurus of Odissi
were Gotipuas in their young days.
