THANG-TA, PUNG CHOLOM
Pung Cholom (Manipuri Drummer) is a traditional Dance of Meiteis
(Manipuris) and performed during the ceremonial functions. The
beauty of the movement of this Dance is each dancer carries a pung, and plays on it as he dances. This vigorous dance is performed only by
man and there is a stricking synchronisation of the beat of the drum
and movement of the feet executed in intricate pattern.
