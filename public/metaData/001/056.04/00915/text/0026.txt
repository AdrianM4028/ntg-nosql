Kol Kali
Kol Kali is a joyous dance performed to the accompaniment of songs
sung by the leader first and repeated next by the dancers in a chorus.
The dancers, holding short sticks dance in large, clear and very
definite steps. The songs full of gusto include a great deal of alliteration
and contain many Arabic, Urdu and Tamil words.
