Ch hei lam
People squat around in a circle on the foor, sing to the beat of a drum
or bamboo tube while a pair of dancers stand in the middle, recite the

song and dance along with the music. The dance often takes place over a
round of rice beer in the cool of the evening. The lyrics are spontaneous
on-the-spot compositions recounting the heroic deeds and escapades of
heroes and they also praise the honoured guest present in their midst.

Dhimsa

The womenfolk of the hill tribes living in the Araku valley of the
Vishakhapatanam district of Andhra Pradesh perform this dance.
Generally the women belonging to the Porja caste perform Dhimsa at
local fairs and festivals. It is believed that if the dance is performed,

family life will be blissful and without any tribulation.
