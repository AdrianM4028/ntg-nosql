Tamang Selo
Tamang selo is a Sikkimese folk dance patronised by the 'I‘amangs.
This dance is also known as Damphu. Damphu is the traditional musical
instrument of the Tamangs.

Tamang selo is one of the most spell-binding dances depicting the
colourful life style of the hill people which finds expression in the
splendour of their festivals and dances.
This dance is full of fun and vigour and hence it is performed during
Dasain or Dussehra.

Thus the Tamang dance or Damphu dance has since time immemorial
been a traditional dance performed by young and old folk alike in their
traditional costumes.
