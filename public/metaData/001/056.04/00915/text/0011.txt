Bagstonrches
The Ladakhi Wedding Dance ‘Bagstonrches’ is performed on the

occasion of marriages and continues for weeks. This is a fascinating
dance from beyond the Himalayas. Wearing sumptuous costumes and
carrying various items of worship, the dancers move gracefully,
imparting to the dance a lyrical charm. Although executed in a slow
tempo the body movements are powerful and precise.
Performed by Mipam 0tsal and team members, Leh, Jammu & Kashmir
