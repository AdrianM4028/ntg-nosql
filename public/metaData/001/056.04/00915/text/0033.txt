Siddi
The Siddis are of African origin and they came to India in the middle
of the twelfth century. In 1100 AD, a Siddi kingdom was established on
the western coast.

The dance of the Siddis have retained some of their original African
character and are specially noted for their complex rhythm and virile
martial movements. The accompanying music is simple but percussive
and syncophated.
