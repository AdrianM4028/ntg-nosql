Mahasu Nati
Nati dances are performed in varying. styles all over Hirnachal
Pradesh. The present districts of Shimla and Solan are known as
Mahasu. In this region, Nati is traditionally performed near the temple

precincts by both men and women. Dancers hold hands dance in circles,
and more in curves and serpentine patterns. In a traditional performance. any one is welcome to join the line, without breaking the rhythm.
While percussion music is provided by Dhol, Nagara, Damdama and
Hodki, the melodies are accompanied by Shehnai, punctuated by
Karnal and Ransingha.
Performed by Debu Ram and team members, Chaupal, Himachal Pradesh.
