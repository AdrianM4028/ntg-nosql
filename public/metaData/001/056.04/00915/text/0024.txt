Jhamakada

Jhamakada is a group dance performed in Kangra. This dance is
exclusively performed by women. The dance is accompanied by a
variety of percussion instruments and lyrical songs. Though simple in
movement and footwork, the dance has extremely complex choreographic patterns. The dancers portray house hold problems, hard work in
fields and meetings and separations of lovers.
Performed by Shakambari Ver’ma and team members, Dharamshala, Himachal Pradesh. 1
