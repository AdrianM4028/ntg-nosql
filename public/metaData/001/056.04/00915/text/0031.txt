Purulia Chhau
Emanating from martial practices, Purulia Chhau is a vigorous form
of dance-drama prevalent in West Bengal. The dance numbers draw
their themes from the two great epics, Ramayana and Mahabharta.
Each actor-dancer wears a mask with elaborate headgear.
Purulia Chhau is distinctive by its acrobatic use of the body and by the
detail that is given to the mask worn by the dance. Powerful
movements, an immense concentration and release of energy mark this
dance form.
Performed by Salish Chandra Mahto and team members, Purulia, West Bengal.
