Ghoomar
Ghoomar, associated with festivals like Holi, Gangor Puja, Teej etc.
is performed by girls of both Haryana and Rajasthan. The dance is
accompanied by songs of love, glory, and victory. The dance is marked
for the whirling movements and graceful body ﬂexions.
Performed by Madan Singh Gauda and ream members, Kishangarh, Rajasthan.
