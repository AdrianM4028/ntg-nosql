A0 Naga Dance
This dance is performed by the A0 Nagas. The dancers enter in a
single file to chorus singing. Hop and step movements are followed for
the formation of a circle. As the tempo increases, two concentric circles
are formed with dancers pretending to attack their enemies.
Performed during the annual festival in the month of January, the
dance has no musical accompaniment, but war songs, often called

“Kaobolimshon Usouho Liamsho” are sung which keep rythrn with the
brisk movement of the dancers.
Performed by T. Obang A0 and team members, Mukokchung, Nagaland.
