Dollu Kunitha
Dollu Kunitha is a drum-dance performed by the menfolk of a
shepherd community called Kourkas. This dance from Karnataka is
marked for its powerful drumming and Vigorous dancing which is
replete with acrobatic elements.
Performed by K. L. Vijai and team members, Shimoga. Karnamka.

डोलू बुल्पीठा
