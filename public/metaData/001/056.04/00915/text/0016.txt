The Chadheyia dance is a component of the Dandanata tradition of
Orissa. The leg extensions used in this dance have much similarity with
that of the Mayurbhanj Chhau style. Like Chhau, this dance form has
also elements of martial practices. The accompanying music resembles
that of Chhau.
Performed by Bhagaban Sahu and team members, Ganjam, Orissa.
