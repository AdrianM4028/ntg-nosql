Bihu
Bihu is the most important festival of Assam, especially among the
agricultural communities. There are different types of Bihu performed
at different stages of cultivation and in different seasons. The
participants dance in a circle beginning with a slower tempo which
gradually increases. Musical accompaniment is provided by drums,
cymbals, hornpipes, harps and bamboo clappers. Although the dance is
inspired by agricultural operations the songs and the graceful dancing
build up an atmosphere of love and romance.
Performed by Lakhi Prasad Phukan and team members, Sibsagar, Assam.
