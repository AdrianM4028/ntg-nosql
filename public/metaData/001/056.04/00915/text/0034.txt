Songi Mukhavate
Songi Mukhavate of Maharastra is associated with the worship of
Devi on the Chaitra Purnima. The festival is celebrated after Holi.

The dancers wear masks and perform with sticks and the dance,
always executed in fast tempo, shows the virility and litheness of the
artistes. The role of Narasimha is performed by two dancers.
Performed by Shravan Laxman Gawali and team members, Nasik, Maharashtra.
