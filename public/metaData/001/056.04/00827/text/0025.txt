Ponung
A folk dance of the Adis from Arunachal Pradesh ‘Ponung’ is performed
during the festival and before harvesting, seeking a good harvest and
welfare of the village community.
9

The young married women and girls take part in this dance led by ‘Miri
a man adept in mythical songs about the origin of paddy and other crops.
While the ‘Miri’ sings rattling the iron discs loosely fitted to the hilt of his
sword. the female dancers continue to dance rhythmically singing in a
chorus.
