Phag
As the name of the dance suggests it is danced to celebrate the mellow
month of “Phagun”. Marking the colourful festival of “holi”. The sounds
of “Tasha” “Nagara” and “Dhol” preceed the men who come running and

singing. They dance in a circle acting out the spirit of “holi” with “gulal”
and “pichkaris”. Then the women come in carrying “koraras” which are
knotted lengths of cloth. They run about among the men hitting them with
the “koraras” while the men defend themselves as best as they can with
