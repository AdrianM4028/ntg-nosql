'I‘appate Gundlu
This folk form of Andhra Pradesh uses the rim shaped ‘Kanjira’ to
accompany the drums hung around the dancers’ necks. A strong group of
young and roboust men take part in this vigorous dance singing songs in
the praise of the local goddess. They perform acrobatics to .the melodious
rhythm created by the drums.
