D andiy a R as
R33 is one of the ancient and yet most popular dance form of Gujarat . l ts
origin has been traced to Lord Krishna. The graceful dance of Lord
Krishna with Gopis in Vrindavan is known to all as Krishnaleela. Ras is a
unique synthesis of folk dance, folk art, colour and folk music. Circular

movements with speed and grace are the main features of R35. The roar ol
the Dhol, the colourful gorgeous costumes, speed together with vigour and

gusto of dancers leaves audiences spell bound. The dance, described as
“Hallisak Krida" or “Dand Rasak” in Mahabharata is the same Ras or

Dandiya Ras as we know it today.
