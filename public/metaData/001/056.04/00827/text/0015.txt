Hunta Dance
The hunter dance of Santhals who live in the hilly plateau of Chhota
Nagpur of Santhal Parganas in Bihar, is known as Hunta Dance. It is
performed only by men and is full of strength and vigour. The dance
depicts the act of getting ready for the hunt with bow and arrows, stalking
the animal and finally shooting it. Mime and slow strong stepping,
measured movements and syncopation of the metre of the drum beats, are
distinctive features of this ancient tribal dance.
