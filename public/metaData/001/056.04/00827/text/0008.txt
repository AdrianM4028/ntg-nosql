Birsu Mahashiv Deva dance
The young women and men of Hanol village of district Dehradun
perform this dance during the festival when Lord Shiva is worshipped as
Mahashiv deva. The songs accompanying the dance are in praise of the
honoured deity. Dancers form circles dressed in traditional long white
tunics brandishing swords in an expression of Tandav Bhav.
