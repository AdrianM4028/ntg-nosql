Peacock Dance
Also known as “Poila Kuthneathaim” this is ajoyous dance from Tamil
Nadu associated with rejocing during the harvest festival ‘Pongal’. Artists
mimic the movements and mannerism of the Peaccock and are usually part
of a larger presentation featuring other animals like the bull and bear as
well as the dummy horse.
