'I‘hang Ta
Thang—ta is the martial art of Mainpur practised with sword and spear.
Training in this art, associated with myths of creation, is rigourous and
begins early. Several forms of dance in Manipur have an affinity to
Thang-ta. Indeed all dance movements of the ‘Meitri‘ people, are said to
have originated from this martial art and are linked to the snake lore of
Manipur. The movements with the Thang (Sword) are to ward off evil
spirit. while the Ta (spear) is held in a position to protect. Apart from its
ritualistic character, Thang-ta also provides basic training in warfare,
therefore the kings of Manipur maintained Thang-ta experts in their
Courts.
