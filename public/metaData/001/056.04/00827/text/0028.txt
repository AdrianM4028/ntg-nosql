TarangmeI
On the occasions of ‘Dussehra‘ and ‘Holi’ the youth of Goa swarm the
streets in colourful groups waving flags and streamers [tarang] inspiring
and inviting one and all to imbibe the festive spirit. The spirited youth
shout Ho ! Ho ! to the beat of ‘Romut‘, ‘Dhol’ and ‘Tasha’ adding
excitement to the colourful atmosphere created by the rainbow like
costumes of the dancers and the varied hues of the flags and streamers they
carry.
