KUNTI
Kunti's real name was Prithaa, the Earth. As a young princess she obtained a boon
of a Mantra (Incantation) from sage Durvasa by which she would be able to summon
any god to appear. To test the efficay of the boon she invoked the resplendant Sun God
who appeared but would not leave without uniting with her. She had no choice but to
submit. She extracted a promise from the Sun God to restore her maidenhood each time
the Mantra was used.
Kunti was married to Pandu, the Kuru Prince, who was under a curse that he

would die at the moment of love. Kunti did not lose heart. Using her intense willpower
and gift of Invocation, she summoned the Gods: Yama, Vayu and Indra, by whom she
begot three noble sons, Yudhisthira, Bheema and Arjuna. She thus enhanced the prestige
of her family in the face of adversity, and ensured the continuation of Pandu's dynasty.
Kunti is thus venerated for her indomitable will and boundless forbearance.
