MAND ODARI
The name Mandodari denotes tender feminine beauty but who also had strength
of mind.
I
Mandodari was the queen of Ravana, the mighty king of the Asuras who ruled
over Lanka. AlthOugh surrounded by aggresive and arrogant men of the Asura race. Mandodari retained the purity of her character, heart and spirit, and her Atman (Spirit)

shone with fierce luminosity cutting through the obscuring dross. She repeatedly
scolded her husband for foollishly abducting Sita, and foretold the dire consequences
that would befall Ravana, his kingdom of Lanka, and the entire Asura race. Yet she

remained his companion in adversity, without compromising her dignity or her
innermost convictions.
Mandodari is thus venerated for purity of purpose, and a strongly centered being.
