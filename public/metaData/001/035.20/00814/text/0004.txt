' 'I’ANCHA-KANYAA' '
Since the beginning of Times till the present age, the infinite creative cycles of the
Primal Energy have continued endlessly. At every moment in Time, in every galaxy and
solar system, countless animate beings are born and sustained by the grace of this Divine

Energy. Small incidents as well as great sagas take place, the Wheel of Time continues
to rotate endlessly.

In every Age, the elements of Darkness and Ignorance have been destroyed again
and again by the luminosity of the Divine Energy. In the Hindu shastras, the force that
destroys Ignorance has been designated as the female element of the Divine Energy, and
has been given the symbolic name "Kanyaa".
A Kanyaa is not necessarily a Kumari (maiden). A Kanyaa is a woman whose Spirit

is Incorruptible and fully Autonomous. A Kanyaa may be married, sometimes to more
than one husband, a part of the Cosmic Design. Even within their lifetimes, such women

give lesser mortals the chance to experience the Immeasurable and the Infinite.
In India, there are five such kanyaas whose names are invoked with reverence. In

this evening's presentation, an attempt is made to present a small glimpse into their lives
and characters, with the hope that it will inspire today's Indian women to become
stronger and will emancipate the men.
In this presentation, the Bharatanatyarn and Odissi styles of dance and music have
been employed. The sundry characters relevent to the tales of the five Kanyaas have been
indicated by the use of shadow-puppets. All the naratives are based on the primal-texts.
