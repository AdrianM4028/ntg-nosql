COSTUME D ES IG N E R

Meeta Mishra
Meeta Mishra graduated from the NSD in 2005 with
a specialization in Design and Direction and went
on to receive training at the NINASAM Theatre
Institute in Karnataka. Over the past few years she
has designed costumes and sets and helped in the
direction of plays. In the past she has worked with
the Bread and Puppet Theatre in New York and with
the Karnataka State Repertory, RANGAYANA, for
three consecutive years, where she contributed
greatly with her organizational skills during the
group's annual national theatre festival. At present
Ms. Mishra practices theatre in Karnataka and
Delhi.
