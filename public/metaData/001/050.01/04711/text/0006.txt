Pt. Satya Dev Dubey
Pt. Dubey was born in 1936 in Bilaspur (Madhya
Pradesh). He started his theatre career at a

young age while still atcollege. He was inspired
to work in the field of drama by Smt. Uma
Anand and Vijay Anand. Two major inﬂuences
who shaped his attitude towards the theatre are
I’arshwanath Atlekar and RC. Shenoy. Pt.
Dubey started his active career as a director in
1956. , Eminent modern playwrights such as

' Badal Sircar, Vijay Tendulkar, Mohan Rakesh,
Girish Karnad, Adya Rangacharya and Dharma
Vir Bharti as well as many western playwrights
have been produced by Pt. Dubey. Pt. Dubey
also directs plays in Marathi with equal
distinction.
Some of his highly acclaimed productions are
Andha-Yug, Adhe—Adhure, Hayavadan, Achha
Ek Baar Aur, Sakharam Binder, Pratibimb,

Virasat, Evum Inderjit, Educating Rita, Dear
Liar. Pt. Dubey's contribution to Hindi theatre
is significant for the success it received in a
predominantly non-Hindi speaking area.
Pt. Satya Dev Dubey has also directed some of
the new wave Hindi films. He received the
Sangeet Natak Akademi Award in 1971.
