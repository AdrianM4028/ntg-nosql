Choreograher
Trained in France in Fold dance, Modern Jazz, Ballet and contemporary, he has
also been exposed to different kinds of ballrood : give, waltz, tango and salsa.
Based in India for the last seven years, he studied Chhau Mayurbhonj under the
guidance of Shashidharan wair from Shriram Bharatiya Kala Kendra.
Involved in numerous programmes all over India, he has been performing as a
dancer and actor and choreographing shows in different backgrounds and various
vacabularies : dance shows in ball room, contemporary, Fusion and classical forms,
theatre, Fashion, television, Cinema and concept performances for the corporate
world.
