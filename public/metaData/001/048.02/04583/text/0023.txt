Costume Desinger
Born in April, Ms. Banerjee is a graduate in English. She is also a graduate of
National School of Drama (1998-2001), specializing in acting. She has worked with
eminent theatre personalities of India and abroad. Before coming in NSD, she was
associated with Padatik, Kolkata.
She is interested in Costume Design since her school days. It has become her
passion during her college days. Got focused in it at NSD and trying to experiment
on different styles. Presently she is working as an artist of National School of
Drama Repertory Company.
