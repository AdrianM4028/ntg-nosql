Dilli Chalo
The story of the play is about the seven guerrillas of I.N.A. on a mission to blow up a British
aerodrome in Palel, Manipur during the Second World War at Imphal frontier. In this play,

the mission commander, Prodyot, works according to a secret plan drawn up by none other
than Netaji himself. Even Prodyot’s associates are totally unaware about it. In the mean time
the British army discovers the secrets and arrests Prodyot and tortures him severely in
order to know the whereabouts of Netaji. Finally, Prodyot does not bear the torture and

discloses it to them. His associates are deeply hurt and angered by his apparent treachery.
However, the climax reaches when the INA. guerrillas successfully destroy the airfield
and Netaji remains untraced by the British army.
