ABOUT THE WRITER
Sanjay Sahay, one of India's well renounced writer born on
21st oct.1958 in Bihar. Graduated in Commerce, a Cultural
Activist Known for long stories and keeps on Writing
Articles, stories for magazines and newspapers. He has
been awarded by ' Rajat Karnal ' award for the film ' PATANG ' .
