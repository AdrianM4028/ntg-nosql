Natyotsava ‘05
The N80 Repertory visits Bangalore after over a decade to stage some of their best-loved

plays in Hindi in Natyotsava ‘05, a ﬁve-day theatre festival at the Chowdaiah Memorial Hall
from October 17 to October 21 2005.
Besides bringing these productions before the Bangalore audience, Natyotsava ’05 also
aims at generating funds for Kadambari’s project to adopt Shivarpatna village.
The productions being staged are

Taj Mahal ka Tender - October 17
Anarndas ka Potha - October 18
Jane-Maan - October 19
Us Cheez ke En Amne Samne - October 20
Ghasiram Kotwal - October 21
