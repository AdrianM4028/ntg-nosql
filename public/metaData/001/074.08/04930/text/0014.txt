THE PLAY
Two typists in Connaught Place, Delhi, grow old
together while typing addresses on postcards. Their
window to the world is, literally. a window that looks

out into Connaught Place. They stand at the win—
dow, disparage the world, and reassure themselves

that their lives carry more meaning than those ofpeople outside.
