Sharon Lowen is a leading performing artist of three
forms of Indian Dance Odissi, Chhau and Manipuri.

A Fulbright and American Institute of Indian Studies
Smithsonian scholar with an MA. in Dance from the
University of Michigan, Sharon has contributed to the
appreciation and understanding of Indian Dance
traditions with scholarly articles and hundreds of
lecture demonstrations and concerts at universities,

schools, museums and art centres and as visiting
professor at universities in United States and India.
