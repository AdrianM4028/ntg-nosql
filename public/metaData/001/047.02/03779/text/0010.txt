How a creative artist makes his way through the obstacles
of life? How he chooses his own form of expression and
how he copes with the challenges of his own creation and

society as well? These are the questions I have always been
pondering upon for a long time. This is not a new thought.

Even Mohan Rakesh's 'Ashadh Ka Ek Din', Jagdish
Chandra Mathur's 'Konark' and Bhisham Sahani's
'Hanoosh' deal with the same questions in their own way.
Sanieev's recent novel Sutradhaar on Bhikari's life also
underlines the ups and downs of an artist's life.
