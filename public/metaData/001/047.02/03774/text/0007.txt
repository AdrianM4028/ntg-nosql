Born in 1972, Asif is a resident of Khaira a small
suburb of district Chhapra, in Bihar. He has

completed his post-graduation in Geography
from Patna University. In the year 2000, he

completed his diploma in Acting from the
National School of Drama. He has worked
under renowned Directors as an Actor in more

than thirty plays.

Along with acting, Asif himself has directed
more than 10 plays and has designed light for
quite a number of plays. He has worked as an
actor in the National School of Drama
Repertory Company also. The adaptation of
’Anamdas Ka Potha’, (a play by the NSD
repertory group) is by Asif himself. He has a
ﬂare for writing too. ’Bawraman Dekhne Chala
Ek Sapna’ is his pen-child. He has also scripted
’Anahad Yatra’ for the Bhutan Festival. Plays
like ’Kya Jhoothe The Ve Sheeth’ and ’Kashmir’
have been translated by him. He also did
dramatization of the novel ”Don Quixote” of

Miguel-de-Cervantes as “Mast Kalandar” for
the third year student production of NSD
directed by Mr. Bansi Kaul. In the year 2006 he
has participated in writers workshop
conducted by Royal Court London. Presently
he is working on a script started during the
workshop which will be produced in January
2007.
