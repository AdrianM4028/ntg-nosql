MESSAGE

I am glad to know of the progress

made by the Theatre Centre (Calcutta) in
organising their Annual Festival of Plays.
I am particularly glad that plays in a
number of Indian languages as well as in
English are produced by them during this
Festival.
I wish them success.

हुँफ्रें८३७ग्न७९८८/८५८3/3र्ण७८3ष्टि५

New Delhi,
April 16, 1957.
