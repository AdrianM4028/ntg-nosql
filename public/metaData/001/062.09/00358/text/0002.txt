"HUMSUB" is a drama group established by the Cultural
Committee of Ghalib Institute.
Its aim is the develOpment of
Urdu plays and theatre and growth of a strong tradition of Urdu
dramatics in India.
There has been a rich tradition of Urdu
theatre, languishing due to lack of patronage and nourishment.

In post-independence era "HUMEUB" is the first organised effort
to restore the glory of Urdu theatre.

Humsub Drama Group was founded on 3th August, 1974 by
Begum AbidaAhmed who is its Chairman.
