the characters, the situations as well as the locales have been changed
so as to suit the Dogra milieu into which the play has been transformed.
The sequences of witches have been transformed into the Dogra ritual
of Iadiyaan and the predictions are done by Jogi and Masan in order
to give the play a local ﬂavour. The original tunes of Dogri folk form
Baaran have been utilized in somewhat stylized manner, using a chorus
instead of a single singer. The performance has been conceived and
executed in the manner of a Fringe Production.
