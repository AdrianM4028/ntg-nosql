DIRECTOR NOTE
The play “Dil Te Dewaar” is an experimental and symbolic in nature. The drama potrays the story of an
unconscious person who is forced and suppressed by the vallanish forces around him in the society.
The Director of the play tries his best to apply the modern techanies and skills for the development of this
interesting story. Moreover the said play is based on the new trends of the world theatre. Many more

experiments have been made by the director to boost up the idea created by the writer.
The drama will be liked and enjoyed by all types of viewers for its wit and hilarious characters. All
characters in this play are fictitious and writer’s own creation. Coincidence of any event or dialogue or
situation with reality is mere change.
