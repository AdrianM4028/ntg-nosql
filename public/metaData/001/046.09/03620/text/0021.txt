Translator

Arvind Kumar

Arvind Kumar was born on 17 Jan 1930 and received a post graduate
degree in English. Author of the Hindi Thesaurus 'Samantar', writer,

poet, critic, journalist, assistant editor ('Sarita' and 'Caravan' Magazines)
editor (Madhuri, Readers Digest), Arvind Kumar has been associated
with theatre since 19505. He began his journey in theatre as backstage
member in Delhi Art Theatre, Delhi and then moved on to Mumbai. He
prepared the Hindi Version ofJulius Caesar while on rest from a Heart
Ailment during the compilation of the Hindi measures. The play version
was directed by Shri Alkazi in NSD in 1992
