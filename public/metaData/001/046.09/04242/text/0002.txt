Here is shown before the public the history
of the troubled reign of Edward the second, King of
England, and his lamentable death/likewise the glory and
end of his favourite, Gaveston / further the disordered fate
of Queen Anne / likewise the rise and fall of the great earl
Roger Mortimer / all which befell in England, and specially
in London, more than six hundred years ago.....
