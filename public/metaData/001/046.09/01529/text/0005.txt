Robin Das, born 19 May 1951.
He graduated with Economics
Honours from Utkal University in
1973. In 1976 graduated from
the National School of Drama
and in 1978 was appointed
lecturer, Scenic Design there.
Das was one of the
founder-members of Hum
Theatre Group, Delhi, (1976—83)

was a fellow on a research
project: Interaction between
Modem Theatre and Traditional
Theatre, sponsored by the Ford
Foundation (1984—85).
