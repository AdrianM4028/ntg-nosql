The Director
A NSD alumnus, Kirti Jain has acted in and directed
several plays, has contributed articles in Hindi and English
to theatre related books and journals; produced plays and
documentaries for Doordarshan and is Trustee, Natarang

Pratishthan, a theatre resource centre and Archives. She
joined the NSD as Faculty in 1977 and went on to become
the School's Director from 1988—1995. During her
directorship Prof. jain started the TIE Company, the
documentation and publication programmes and the
Regional Research Centre ofNSD.
Prof. Jain's directorial work ranges over several productions
that include Hayavadana with English students at
Dartington College of Arts, Am Kitne Tukdc on Partition,
Tinka Tinka, Sab Kuch Chakachak, Baghdad Burning, and Ek

Ruka Hua Faisla amongst others. In 2010 Prof. Kirti Jain was
honoured with the B.V Karanth Srnriti
Puraskar.
