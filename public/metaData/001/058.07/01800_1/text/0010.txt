NATYA SAMARO H '90
Natya Sarnaroh ’90 is the seventh annual festival of
plays presented in Delhi by Sangeet Natak Akademi.
\ It features five new productions in Hindi, Tamil,
Oriya, Assamese and Marathi earlier staged in Zonal
Theatre Festivals at Vijayawada, Chandigarh,
Calcutta and Aurangabad. The present festival, and
८ the Zonals preceding it, are products of the scheme
of Assistance to Young Theatre Workers, supporting
experimental work by young directors.
