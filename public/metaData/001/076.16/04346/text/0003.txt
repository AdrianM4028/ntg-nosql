From its very inception Rang Vidishak has
been involved with children's plays like Rang
B/range Jutey, and over time we have
expanded to work with the children of
prisoners, policemen, those belonging to
jhuggis and small schools, all with the aim of
exploring new methodologies in acting and
developing new scripts. Our aim is to achieve
this goal in practical as well as theoretical
terms, and this play is a step in that direction.
