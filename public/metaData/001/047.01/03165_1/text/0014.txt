Born in Ajmer. Rajasthan in the year 1 940, Mo han

Maharishi began his career in drama with All India
Radio , Jaipur in the year 1955-56 as an
amateuractor. He took active interest in college
dramatics and as Cultural Secretary of his college,
participated in National Youth Festivals to win
several prizes as actor/director.

It was in the year 1962 that he decided to turn
professional and joined the National School of
Drama as a student. There he came in contact
with the most eminent teacher/director in lnclia,

Mr E. Alkazi. He graduated from the National
School of Drama & Asian Theatre Institute in the
year 1965 with Girish Ghosh and Bharatawarcls

and was offered a two year stint in the National
School of Drama Repertory Company.
Since 1967, Mohan Maharishi has held several

important positions in the ﬁeld of theatre. As a
freelance teacher/director, he worked in many

Indian universities before taking a job (Producer,
Drama and Current Affairs) in Doordharshan,
Delhi. He married Anjala Chitnis, a trained actress

from NSD and a professional classical dancer.

ln 1973, the couple got their first major
assignment abroad. The Government of India sent
them to Mauritius as Theatre Experts where they

together initiated a Youth Theatre Movement.
Mohan Maharishi was further retained as Cultural
Aclv i ser to the Prime Minister by the Government

of Mauritius for a period of three years.

He returned to India in 1979 to head the
Department of Drama, Punjab University,
Chandigarh. Considering his eminence in the
ﬁeld, he was directly taken on as Professor, a job
that he still retains.
