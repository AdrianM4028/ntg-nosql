R o b i n D as

Born in 1951 in a village in Orrisa, he studied science
and economics before entering the National School of

Drama, New Delhi, for professional training in theatre
with a specialization in design. Founder and artistic
director of FAM IT (Forum for Assimilation of Modern

Indian theatre), he is known for directing experimental
theatre and creating designs for unusual performance

space. Currently Professor of Scenic Design at the
National School of Drama, a self taught sculptor and
visual artistic working in ink, water, color and oils. He
is continuously seeking a multidisciplinary approach to

training in the plastic and performing arts while
assirnilating the ancient and traditional with the
contemporary and post modern.
