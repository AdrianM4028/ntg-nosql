Malini Chari has been performing in All India Radio as well as in music concerts
from a very young age. She started learning music from Rattan Singh ji of Delhi Gharana

and now is the disciple of renowned vocalist of Gwalior Gharana Pandit Laxman Krishna
Rao Pandit. Malini possesses a melodious voice.
The salient features of her style are rendering of the khayal in the typical Gwalior
style, development of the composition in sathang gayaki.

M. Phil. in Delhi University, Malini was awarded a prize in All India
