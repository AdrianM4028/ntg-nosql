Presentation : EK DUNI EK

Arvind Pandey was born in Kumaon
Hills; completed schooling and college from
Dehradun. Joined National School of Drama,
and specialised in acting in 1991. Done one
year research fellowship on 'Method Acting‘
from N .S.D. Acted in major roles in several
plays with the eminent directors from India
& abroad. Directed number of plays in
different languages, some of them are'Death in Instalments',

'The Proposal',

'Kabhoon Na Choden Khet' & 'The Typists'.
Conducted theatre workshops for children
and adults in different parts of the country
and also worked with the handicapped and
orphan children. Taught and directed at the
'Department of Theatre and Television,
Punjabi University, Patiala. Visited UK. as
a part of research project on Acting. Presently working independently in theatre.
He takes the Sahitya Kala Parishad
stage tonight to present his play in the 9th
Yuva Mahotsava.
