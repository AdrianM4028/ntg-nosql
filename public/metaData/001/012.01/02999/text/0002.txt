About the Director
Sri Urmil Kumar Thapliyal, one of the best
known theatre personalities of Uttar Pradesh, has
taken a lot from folk and regional theatre.

Recepient of may awards at State and National
Level, he has been involved in bringing music centre stage while reviving ‘Nautanki' tradition. He
contributed a lot in developing theatre in various
dialects viz Bhundel Khandi, Awadhi and Braj.
The well known plays directed by Sri Thapaliyal
are Harishchandra Ki Larai, Bhaonri Katha, Jas
unke din Bahure.

Folk Style : Garhwali Theatre
Although there is no distinct folk art form
such as Garhwali Theatre, but over the years, by

incorporating Garhwali folk songs, dances and folk
tales in the modern theatre form, Garhwali Theatre has come to be recognised as an emerging art
form. Each year the UP. Govt. has been organising
Uttaranchal Natya Mahotsav. This has given

great fillip to Garhwali Theatre specially to
Garhwali play-wrighting. The incorporation of
typically Garhwali songs, dances, folk tales and
rituals has given these plays a distinctive regional
flavour.
