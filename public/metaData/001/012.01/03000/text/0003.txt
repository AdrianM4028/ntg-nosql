Mukti Mantra-the story of the play
‘Mukti Mantra’ is based on a folk tale prevalent among the Tharu tribals. It seeks to expose
the exploitation of simple tribal folk by outsiders.
The Tharu tribals have blind and naive faith which
is sometimes exploited by non-tribal people for
monetary gain.

The story commences with the startling news _
that in the nearby forest the statue of Lord Shiva
has suddenly appeared. The hard working and contented but immensely religious Tharus rush to the
spot to get Darshan of Lord Shiva. They ﬁnd that
next to the statue of Lord Shiva is an unknown
‘Baba’, i.e., a mendicant. The innocent tribals start
praying to Lord Shiva and invoke his blessings to
protect their forest, meadows, animals and crops.

As time passes the mendicant makes many dis-

ciples who start living with him. The simple and
hard working tribals continue their worship of the
statue of Lord Shiva.
