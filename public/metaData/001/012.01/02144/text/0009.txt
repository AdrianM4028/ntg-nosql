After being victorious in the War of Kalinga, Ashok starts thinking himself as an Almighty and Controller of the whole Universe.
The play propounds the doctrines enunciated by Lord
Budha. In the second half of the play the message for liberating
the mankind condemned to constant suffering and sorrows is
given by Baudha Bhikshu. ln the last scene of the play this Bhikshu
becomes able in vanquishing the pain and torture inflicted in
the hell by virture of his self confidence and equanirnity of mind
and changes the dreadful atmosphere of the hell into a calm,

serene and fearless atmosphere. Thus Priyadarshi Ashok is impressed with the doctrines of Lord Budha. His heart Changes
and he takes pledge to serve the humanity in the righteous way.
Though the theme of the play is based on a historical char—
acter yet the play in the recent times is very relvant.
This is one of the war series play which conveys the message of non-violence and peace very successfully when the whole
humanity is standing on the verge of self destruction.
