जाल्क : बाबूजी

B.V. Karanth the director of the play ‘Babuji’ was born at
Bapukori, Karnatak in the year 1929. He was engaged in service
with a Commercial ‘Theatre Company Gubbi’ in his early child—
hood days.

In the meantime he continued his education and

from National School of Drama, Sri Karanth organised many
theatre workshops in various parts of the country and directed
many well known plays. He became the Director, National School
of Drama in the year 1977 and remained over there till 1981.
Besides he had been Director Repertoire, Bharat Bhavan, Bhopal

and Director, ‘Rangayan’, Repertory of Karnatak State. Sri
Karanth has been awarded Sangeet Natak Akaderny Award in
the Year 1976 and “Padma Shree” Award in the year 1981.
Sri Karanth has directed series of plays. Well known among
these plays are - HayaVadan, Barnam Van, Chandra Gupta,
Skanda Gupta, Andher Nagri. Sri Karanth has also made a significant contribution to the growth of children’s theatre in the
