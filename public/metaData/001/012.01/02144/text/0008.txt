Ratan Thiyam
Born in 1948 Ratan Thiyarn graduated from National
School of Dram in the yar 1974. Direction of 50 plays and writing
of 5 full length plays goes to his credit. He has contributed a lot in
enriching the Modern Indian Theatre by the elements of folk culture of North-Eastern in States of India. After establishing the
‘Chorus Repertory Theatre’ at Irnphal he has directed will known
plays like Urubhangam, Chakravyuh, ImphaI-Imphal , Uchek Leng
Medang,, and won the Iaurels throughout the country and abroad.
Chakravyuh was first staged on the occasion of Sangeet Natak
Akaderny Drama Festival in the year 1984. This play was awarded
‘Fringe First’s’ Award in the year 1987 at Edinborough Cornrnonwealth Theatre Festival.
Ratan Thiyarn was awarded sangeet Natak Akaderny Award
in the year 1987 and Padma Shree Award in the year 1989. Sri
Thiyarn was Awarded Nandikar Award in the year 1992. Plays
directed by Sri Ratan Thiyam have been staged in the countries
like Rome, U.S.S.R, Mexico, Japan, France and Thailand as well.

He has received Fellowship and special invitation from Korean
and Japan Foundation.

Chorus Repertory Theatre
The Chorus Repertory Theatre was established at Imphal
in the year 1976. The repertory was set up with the objective to
enrich the modern Indian theatre with the elements of folk culture of North-Eastern states of India. This repertory is functioning
like an institution to give training to young artistes in the field of
direction, acting, stage craft etc. Various play productions of the
reportory have been taken in high esteem both at national and
international levels.
