The Bhartendu Natya Academy: Bhartendu Academy of
Dramatic Arts, when it was founded in 1975 (with Raj

the large Hindi-speaking belt of Bihar, Uttar Pradesh,
Madhya Pradesh and Rajasthan. At BASA, Raj Bisaria,
from 1 975 to 1 986, shouldered the total responsibility for
teaching acting and direction (aspects), both theoretical

and practical in Western drama from the Greeks to
modern times; theatre criticism adjudication; and
aesthetics of light, sound and set designs. He worked
again as Director of the Academy as well as the Director
of its Repertory Company from 1989 to 1992 and again
from 1995to 1997.
