Between 1585 and 1592 Shakespeare began a successful career in
London as an actor, writer, and part owner of the playing company, The
Lord Chamberlain's Men, later known as the King's Men. He appears to
have retired to Stratford around 1613, where he died three years later.
Few records of Shakespeare's private life survive.
