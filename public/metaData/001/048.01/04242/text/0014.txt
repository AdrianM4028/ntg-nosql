After completing his Masters from the Allahabad University, Shri Neelabh
worked as a producer for the Special Broadcast Service of the BBC. He began
writing while he was still a student and his first work was published during the
initial years of his professional life. In 1984 he returned to India and began writing
on a full—time basis. Today he is recognized as one -of the most proficient and

talented writers of Hindi literature. Shri Neelaabh has written several
screenplays for films and television as well.
