The most interesting side of the script, prepared with an
interweaving of three stories and some poems of Santosh Chaube,
is its presentation. An experimental presentation with a new theme
makes Santosh’s stories distinctive ones. He has tried to explore
the intimate relationships between man and woman on one side
whereas he has given the psychological analysis of every
character with fullest of his involvement, on the other side. With its
progression the story keeps on raising some questions. Being
lonely in the society the man has to bear many relationships
knowingly or unknowingly. Every relationship has its own
expectations. These expectations and hopes have no logic but only
the boundaries that these have created for their selves. Audio
visual medium has also been experienced in the presentation and
the decisions on sensitive subjects have been left upon the
audience.
