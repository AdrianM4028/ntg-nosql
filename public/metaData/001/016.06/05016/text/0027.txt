The plays ‘Rise and Shine' and ‘Medea' are both one woman shows
which are written by Nobel Prize winner Italian Dario F0 and his famous
actress wife Frank Rame. On the basis of these two plays, the shows
depict multitude of maternal emotions.
At the outset the play Rise and Shine seems to be a comedy. But the
essence of the play depicts the faces of a woman as a wife, mother,
servant and owner. lt portrays the manner in which a woman burdened '
with different responsibilities draws herself to fulﬁll the expectations of
modern society. The play puts forth this grave issue.
The Greek play ‘Medea’ was a controversial play even during the period
of Greek playwright Euripides. From the past 2000 years, this work has
been analysed, researched indepth in different languages of the world. It
has also been depicted in variety of forms and styles which has resulted
in different interpretations.
Dario F0 and Frank Rame have tried to depict this play from a feminist
Marxist deconstructive point of view. The act of Medea killing her
children, symbolically represents the protest against those who instigate .
the system to tyrannies women.
In every human being, there is a hidden voice of dissent. The system is
engrossed in maintaining a stationary state permanently and the
individual desires to bring about change against this situation.
in my view point, the voices of the system also originates from Medea.
This play projects the conﬂict between the society which wants Medea to
be submissive and the revolutionary ideas of Medea.
