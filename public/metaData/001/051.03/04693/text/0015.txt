Berharnpore Chharpatra is a children's theatre group working
solely for the mental and physical development of children.
The group attempts its best to reach them the world of
imagination and fantasy. Since 1993 the group has been able
to stage more than ten productions for the children only in
several parts of West Bengal.
It is a matter of great pride and pleasure that the group's
popular production, Tupur-O-Sa/‘tan, won the best award in
the All Bengal Children Theatre Competition, held at Kalna in
Burdwan district recently. The group also has been invited by
the “West Bengal Natya Academy” to stage its productions for
a couple of times. Apart from this play, the group has a
number of productions to its credit like Buddhu Bhutum,
Swap/70 Bandhu,Pashanpur/, Habu-Gabur Pa/‘la, Darabazi,
Boka Manuser Gappo,Bobby, Jadur Tu/i etc.

Bobby is the play that talks about a girl child's imagination
and fantasy. The story says that Bobby is the only daughter of
her parents, who give her everything except their company.
When her school is over, she returns home and does
everything independently — takes the keys from her
neighbour, opens the door and starts playing with her
dolls
and toys. Though she misses her parents many times,
only
dolls and toys become her company all the way.
Apart from
this, she sometimes takes the wings of imagination
and dives
into the world of fantasy.
