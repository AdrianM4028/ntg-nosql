IIII'BGIIII'S Note

Born in 1968 at Mandi in Himachal Pradesh, Seerna Sharma is
a postgraduate. For last 10 years, she has been associated
with children‘s theatre as both writer and director. She has
conducted several children theatre workshops in different
parts of the state and directed more that 15 plays for
children. She has also worked with eminent theatre
personalities like Devendra Raj Ankur, Prasanna, BV Karanth,
Dinesh Khanna, Suresh Sharma, Pratibha Matkari, Lokendra
Trivedi and Akhilesh Khanna. As an actress, she has
participated in more than forty major plays. She was
adjudged as best actress in the Youth Festival of Himachal
University. She was also honoured the junior fellowship from
Dept. of Culture, Govt. of India in 1999. At present, she is
working as secretary of Himachal Culture Research Forum,
Repertory & Theatre Academy.

I prepared this production with rural children and tried my
best to bind them with the chemistry of love, affection and
brotherhood among themselves and with others. Though the
traditional folklore has been rich in our society, these days it
is decaying gradually. The play is an attempt to revive these
folklores, giving them a new shape. I hope the children can
take more interest on folk tales and educate our society in
some way or the other in future.
