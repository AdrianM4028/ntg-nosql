Dr. Mohan Agashe, a psychiatrist by profession is well known
for his contribution in theatre and films as an actor. He has
been immortalised by his outstanding performance as the
'Machiavellian Nana Pandnavis' in the Marathi musical —
Ghasiram Kotwal.
For the first time, Dr. Agashe has spearheaded Grips Theatre
movement in India for the children and youth. He has directed
more than 12 plays of different Indian languages in this genre,
which has been staged more than 2000 times. He is the
recipient of many awards including Padrnashri and Sangeet
Natak Akademi Award for acting. The President of Germany
also has honoured The Cross of Order of Merit award in 2002
for his contribution to the Grips Theatre for children and youth.
