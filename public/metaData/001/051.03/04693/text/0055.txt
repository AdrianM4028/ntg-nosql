About the Grill"!
The Group, Centre for Education & Voluntary Action, (CEVA),
Chandigarh is involved in initiating and carrying out dialogues
for mutual education, communication and empowerment —
within the community (both rural and urban) — among
different groups like children, teachers, youth, women,
voluntary groups, families, employees etc. For dialogues, the
CEVA reaches out through community theatre, audio-visual
media and films, participatory workshops, interactive
puppetry, story telling and poetry recitation. The search has
been for alternatives to the traditional and frontal modes of
communication (lecture/seminar/speech), and for spaces and
means that are participatory and more empowering for all
individuals involved within families, in classrooms and
organisations.

Danthte Raho talks about the rebukes. Generally more
powerful people in our society have a control over weaker
sections through this means in which the less powerful suffer
always. This play has been evolved out of the outpouring of
the children in the sharing sessions, about their experiences
of living. The combined expression of adults and children,
their insights and contribution is the essence of the play. It
emerges out spontaneously as a subject of concern in the
improvisations that deals with the relationships between
adults and children. The participants explored all the aspects
of rebuke, its expression on humiliation, rejection and insult
that comes with it.
