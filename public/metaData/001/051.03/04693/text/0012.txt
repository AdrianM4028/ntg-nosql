With the race of modernisation and development, I feel, a
man loses his recognition and self. Our rich traditions,
which are the part of everybody‘s life has been decaying
gradually. If we do not make an effort to revive these
traditions, it would completely lose a big part of our folk
culture. It is my best attempt with the children of Bal
Manch to revive the same with this play. It is also an
experiment with the children in which the folk stories of
Malwa, Nirnad and Jhabua are being staged. I tried to use
Malwi, a sweet dialect of the region in this play, to make
it more alive and appealing for the children. I remix
different folk stories with folk music, folk songs and tried
to make it a full-fledged play to link our old tradition with
it.
