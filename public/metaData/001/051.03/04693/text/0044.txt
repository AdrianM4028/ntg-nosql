Born in 1964, Sanjay Kapil, a Graduate from Jammu
University, started his theatre career in 1984 as an actor. He
performed several plays and participated in the National
Drama Festival of Sangeet Natak Akademi, Allahabad,
Chandigarh and Delhi. He is a recipient of junior fellowship
from the Ministry of Human Resources & Development, Govt.
of India. He has directed about 20 plays such as Desh Key
Liye, Chouraha, Poster, Baad Ka Pani, Rakshas, Hatya Ek
Aakar Ki, Taj Mahal Ka Tender, Sakharam Binder, Raja Nanga
etc. As a director he participated in the UP. Rajya Natya
Samaroh, organised by Sangeet Natak Akademi and Sanskriti
Karya Vibag, Kanpur. He got the special prize for direction of
the play, Baad Ka Pani. Recently, he directed Raja Nanga and
received wide response from the audience. Besides, Kapil also
works for the Doordarshan as an approved artist.

The writer has beautifully conceived the play. It was a short
play, but it was developed into a full—fledged one later in the
process of rehearsal. As of today, I feel that every citizen is
facing with the crisis of confidence, and the law of the country
is being misused in some way or the other. This play gives a
confidence to the cornmonmen to think out that we should not
make ourselves to be a part of political or religious crowd, but
to instill in them the courage to root out the corruption that
are flourishing day by day through the nexus between
businessmen and politicians. This theme offers a humorous
treatment on the whole. The music and songs of the play are
interwoven to make the presentation more effective and live.
