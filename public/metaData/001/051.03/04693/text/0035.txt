DATE (Developing Awareness Through Entertainment) is the
group comprises of voluntary theatre workers from different
walks of life. The members also work with several other
professional/amateur groups in Pune and Murnbai. At
present, the group has a 75—member team of playwrights,
directors, music directors, actors, actresses, experts on sets,

lights and costumes, designers, musicians, backstage
workers, managers etc. Grips Theatre is a major activity of
the group and produced several one-act plays related to it. A
month-long festival of plays is also organised in summer
vacations too. Teachers, parents and child psychologists help
in research and follow-up activities. Dr. Mohan Agashe, the
chairperson, guides the group that offers a back up of thinktank during the productions.
For last 10 years, ten plays have been staged in the festivals
organised by various institutions. The group is also associated
with Maharashtra Cultural Centre that organises multi-cultural
activities like dance, drama music, sports, etc.
