Hrishikesh Deahpande has been an active member of the
Grips Theatre movement for last 12 years. He has contributed
a lot in all aspects of theatre activities. Haas/1 Hush Thaa/
Thus/1 is his very first independent directorial venture under
the Grips Theatre Movement. He has already had a couple of
successful one-act plays to his credit.

This play speaks about the ill effects of attitude of the parents
who lack the right kind of communication for their children.
This miscommunication can lead them to the disastrous
future. These ill effects are quite often seen these days as the
pace of life is growing gradually. The parents in this 'modern
age' have to understand the need of initiating communication
and rapport with their children. The children should also know
that they should not run away from difficult situations in life,
and they need to face them with courage by taking help from
the parents.
I found the play apt for today's scenario. With a great help
from Mr. Shrirang, Dr. Agashe and Mr. Amit Patwardhan, this
play has been a success since then.
