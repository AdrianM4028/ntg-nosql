Academy was established in 1986 as an amateur
theatre group at Mandi, Himachal Pradesh. Just after
one year (1987-88), the group has already marked a
tremendous growth in theatrical activities and the first
non-governmental repertory of its kind was set up in
the northern region of India. The objective of the
repertory is to spearhead the cultural movement
through innovative theatre productions in the region.
With all—out effort of the founder director, Suresh
Sharma, a full time one-year residential theatre
academy was also constituted in 1998.
The academy has been fully devoting to the theatre
aspirants from across the country. It provides a
thorough practical as well as theoretical training in
drarnatics. Though it has its own faculty, the academy
tries its best to invite as many visiting faculties as

possible from renowned theatre institutes including
National School of Drama for its further growth. To
facilitate the students and faculty, the academy has its
own campus, research library, and residential hostel for
the outstation students. The repertory also absorbs five
best students from the academy every year.
