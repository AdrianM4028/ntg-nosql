निदेशक (मानदेय)
DATE is a natural fall out of the experiences I had in doing meaningful and socially relevant
realistic theatre for Chhildren and Youth in India in collaboration with GRIPS Theatre from
Berlin and Max Mueller Bhavans (Branches of goethe Institute in India) since 1986.
DATE is an Asian Resource centre for promoting "issue-based” and "Awareness-oriented"
entertainment of high quality for Children and Youth.
DATE will liaison with defferent Theatre groups with the help of its representatives in different
cities to generate good scripts in regional languages, translate them in other languages,
helping theatre groups to produce them and if productions meet the standard required then
promoting their shows. As the show is being produced, DATE will concurrently generate
follow up educational material on the issues raised in the play to be distributed to the children
and teachers. DATE will also organize workshops for teachers and parents for the follow up

activity to be taken up in schools consequent to the shows performed. DATE plans to craeate
information database on Children and Youth Theatre and Script Bank and to keep effective
networking between different theatre groups by publishing news-letter periodically. Anyone
having similar objectives is welcome to have a date with DATE.
