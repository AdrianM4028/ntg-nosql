About the Toli
Sanskaar Rang Toli (T.|.E. Co.) of National School of Drama was
established on October 16,1989 and is one of the important
educational resources in the country. The Toli consists of a group of

actor-teachers working with and performing for children on regular
basis. The plays for children are creative and participatory, specially

designed and prepared for children of different age groups to create
awareness and encourage children to raise questions, make decisions
and adopt theatrical skills as an alternative.
The Toli offers training workshops for teachers to create meaningful
theatre with children and also offers skills and techniques to work in
clasrocrn situations to make learning more effective and injoyable. A
regular annual feature of TI E is a one-month Intensive Summer Theatre
Workshop for Children held in May-June every year. Besides training
in theatre and allied arts. personality development and integration are
important aspects of this workshop. Sunday Club is an extension of
summer workshops, where more intensive work is done with the
children of various age groups to create original plays through a process
of improvisations. Workshops for colleges, social organizations, parents
and children with special needs are also Company's important activities.
Since 1994 the Company has been travelling with its plays and
workshops to various States of the country and has also participated
in International Theatre Festival in Warsaw (Poland).
A Theatre Festical "jashnebachpan" for children and adults was
organized by the Toli in Novembar 1998 inviting plays by & for children
from different parts of the country, which is now a regular annual

feature. In February 2001, the Toll started to organise 'Bal Sangam'
is which about 200-300 child artistes from various regions of the
country participated and ferforrn various performing Folk and
Traditional Arts forms.
