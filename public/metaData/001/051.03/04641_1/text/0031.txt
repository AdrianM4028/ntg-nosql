The Group
Founded in 1960 by a group of young persons
under the leadership of Ajitesh Bandyopadhya,
Nandikar is a theatre group that aims at
producing the best plays and performing them
wherever and whenever possible. During the last
48 years it has produced 80 plays that have been
performed more than 8,000 times all over the
country and abroad. Nandikar also organizes
several festivals,

holds workshops,

aims

at

disseminating theatre related information and is
involved in several socially relevant activities; has
participated in festivals and toured extensively in
India and abroad, and so on.
