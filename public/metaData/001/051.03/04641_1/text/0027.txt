Director's Note
It is difficult to put into words the priceless
experiences gained during my 15 years of
association with Shishu Sarothi. In my career as
a theatre artist I have learnt more from the
children here than what I have taught. They
have completely changed my perceptions and
views about life.
I believe that theatre is a magical inspiration. I
have seen how it unfolds their creativity, gives
them confidence to express themselves, and help
each other remember dialogues and overcome
their shortcomings. In fact theatre/drama is a

therapy which I can say with conviction, will be
used in the future for all children with disabilities.
And the day I see it happen I will feel as if my
journey to success has begun.
The play is not being staged to pass any message to
society. This evening let us simply forget our
worries and laugh as Shishu Sarothi acts out
Buddhmum.
