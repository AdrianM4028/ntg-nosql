The play talks about the intervention of
mankind, as seen in terms of certain moral

values and principles, in the destruction of
living and non—living aspects of the
environment.
Keeping in mind child—spectators, the scenes are
created as a blend of reality and fantasy. The
pace, set and movement in the play aims to
stimulate the spirit of sensitivity in children and
enhance their physical energy as actors within the
limits of the stage. The stage property used in the
play has been made with the help of natural
materials, like the novelist wished, to emphasize
the importance and bio—diversity of nature.
