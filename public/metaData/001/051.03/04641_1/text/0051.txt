Founded with the objective of spreading
awareness of Manipuri martial arts and culture
throughout the world, its artistes have performed
across India and the world. It has established a
performing group for children named 'Blooming
Cultural Academy,' which gives training in
traditional art forms of Manipur under the
guidance of eminent gurus and teachers. During
the past 52 years of its existence, many students
from both India and abroad have come here for
training, and the Association has also opened
branches in some states of India.
