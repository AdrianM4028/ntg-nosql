since early childhood, and involvement with various theatre groups. Acquires a
masters degree in Hindi, moves to Varanasi (Benaras) in pursuit of his interest
in Hindustani classical music and trains for three years under Pandit Omkar Nath
Thaku r.

Graduated from the National School of Drama Delhi, and in the interim
period before finally taking up the position of Director of his alrnarnater, works
actively with Hindi theatre groups to wide critical acclaim, in addition to his

regular assignment as teacher at the Sardar Patel Vidyalaya. Instrumental in
giving a lift to the theatre movement in Bangalore, Bhopal and Heggodu, including
