Ram Rahman

Ram Rahman was educated in Modern School in Delhi where he was an active
member of the Ruchika Theatre Group. He also learned Bharatanatyam with
Kalkshetra Lalita Shastri. He graduated with degrees from MIT and Yale in the
US and has worked as a graphic designer and photographer both in the US and
India for 30 years. Ram was a founding member of the Safdar Hashmi
Memorial Trust, SAHMAT, and has been part ofthe organizing and design team
0fthe pandals on January 1 st for many years. Ramjust held a large retrospective
ofhis photographs, 'Bioscope‘, at the Lalit Kala Akademi in Delhi.
