About the Play
A talented boy Ved? wants to participate in a theatre workshop but workshop organizer rejects him saying
that he is not talented enough to get admission in the workshop. Ved decides to diregt a play to boost up
himself. He inspires his friends to participate in the play. To meet out the expenses and other needs of the
play / production all children contribute their pocket money and take help of their parents. Rehearsals
begin. Initially children and their parents take it lightly due to their ignorance about theatre. But main and
major problem of Ved and his assistant Sanklap is Dabloo, who has been casted as Raja. Whenever he
starts rehearsal he forgets that he is performing the character ठे Raja. He himself becomes Raja and creates
problems. Instead of performing given situation he deviates and threatens other children. Ved, Sankalp
and other children manage it any how. But on the day of grand rehearsal Dabloo creats havoc. He totally
refuse to perform as was decided. He becomes a mad dictator king and orders for Prajamedh Yagya to kill
every citizen including his family members. He wants to enjoy alone the prosperity of his state. All
children start trembling. They cry for help. But ved and sankalp do not loose their sense. They improvise.
They improvise a Mass revolution. . Dabloo' surrenders and realizes that power should be used for the
service to mankind not for destruction.
