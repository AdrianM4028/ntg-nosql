Director Sohaila Kapur Charnaiia is a writer-actress. She worked as a
journalist with the Times of India for several years, developing as a research-

writer. In that span she published a research-based book on the esoteric rites

of the Indian west coast, called Witchcraft In Western India’. Today she is
scripting (and directing) her own television serial, under the banner of Sheii

Kanta Productions, of which Katyayani is the theatre subsidiary. -

She is also writing and publishing fiction, and hopes to publish a book

of her short stories some day.

As an actress she has worked with theatre and ﬁlm luminaries like M.S.
Sathyu, Satyadev Dubey, Nadira Babbar, Amal Allana, Feisal Alkazi, Arun Kuckreja;

Chetan Anand, Shyam Benegal and Govind Nihalani and TN. directors Jalal
Agha, Satish Kaushik, Mohena Singh, Tony and Diya Singh and Shubhdarshini
Singh. She has also anchored several programmes for Doordarshan.
‘Skirmishes' is her third directorial venture on stage; the first two being

‘Miss Julie’ and rTales of the Unexpected’ which she had dramatised ' herself.
