Last year 5000 artists from all corners of the country filled Delhi with
colour, music, dance, drama, flavour and fervour for the first—ever Delhi
Celebrates series of events. The event series, organised to add to the
festivities in the host city of Commonwealth Games 2010, succeeded far
beyond anyone's imagination. Indeed, every event received huge
attendance of the discerning citizens of Delhi.

With a calendar of daily events showcasing the various cultural traditions
and arts of India, Delhi Celebrates offered something for everyone.
