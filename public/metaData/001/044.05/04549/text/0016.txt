ANJANA RAJAN
Anjana Rajan was initiated into Bharatanatayam in Geneva, Switzerland where she
lived as a child. Her teachers here included Srnt. Malik, Srnt. Rajamani and Srnt.
Padmini Mohan-Durr. On completion of her schooling her quest for the authentic
drewAnjana to Kalashetra, Madras, where she pursued a rigorous course of studies
in Bharatanatayarn and allied subjects. Here she came into contact with the
inspiring founder director Rukmini Devi Arundale, whose emphasis on the divinity of
all arts had a deep inﬂuence on her.
Anjana has taught Bharatanatayam and folk dances to children and adults and
conducted mime and movement sessions for theatre artists.
