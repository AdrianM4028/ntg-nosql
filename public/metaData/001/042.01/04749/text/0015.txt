The Director
Heisnam Kanhailal has been active in theatre for the last 40 years as a
playwright, director and actor. Founder-director of Kalakshetra, Manipur,
he has played a pivotal role in the experimental theatre movement, working
to discover an alternative theatre language while using the rich Manipuri
traditional performing arts and culture as a springboard. He has
