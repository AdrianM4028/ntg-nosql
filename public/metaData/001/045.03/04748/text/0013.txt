COSTUME DESIGN
ANILA S. KHOSLA
Anila S. Khosla graduated with theZBest Actress Award from
the NSD in 1977 and until 1981 was member of the NSD Repertory
Company.
While with the Repertory she designedccstumes for several plays
including, ’Mukyamantri’,directed by Ranjit Kapoor, ’The F'ool’
directed by Barry John, ’Surya KiAntim Kiran Se Surya Ki Paha/i
Kiran Tak’ directed by R .G . Bajaj, 'Sandhya Chiraya‘ directed
by Uttara Baoker and ’Kath Na Chooden Khet’, directed by
MK. Raina. Before her departure to New York in 1985, she
performed in a number of plays including several directed
by Messers E. Alkazi, B.V. Karanth, Barry John, MK.
Raina, Ranjit Kapoor RG. bajaj and Fritz Benowitz. She
also appeared in a number of Television Drama's,
including Saba Zaidi's adaptation of Prernchand's
'Zaver Ka Dibba‘ for Doordarshan, and in films. She
has also conducted workshops at the high school
level and taught costume design at Baratendu Natak
Academy in Lucknow.
Since her return from New York in 1998, Anila has
designed the costumes for MK. Raina's ‘Munto
Hazir Hai’ and performed in a number of Hindi
and English plays, including 'Trumpets of
Death' and ‘Grand Finale' directed by Kavita
Nagpal, M.K. Raina's ’Saffr'on Fields',
’Geetanjali in Five Pan‘s’ with Yatrik and
directed by Joy Michael, 'Corpse with Two
Feet' directed by V.K. Shanna, Ranjit
Kapoor's 'Afwah‘ and recently "Larins
Saheb"directed by M. K. Raina.
