Synopsis (Khamosh adalat jari hai)
It is a play within a play. A game is started light heartedly which gradually becomes
macabre ending in
witchhunt .

A group of amateur actors from different walks of life come to a small town
to perform a play. While
waiting for the show they start a mock trail in a spirit of fun. One of the actresse
s Miss Benare is asked
to play the role of the accused and is charged with the crime of infanticide.
At the mock trail the other
actors come to suspect that Miss Benare is carrying a child from a married intellect
ual who is refusing
to take the responsibility. From here the witchhunt starts. The inner jealousy, pettines
s and prejudices of
the group come out in the open. The suspicion is found to be true and the
group also comes to learn of
the identity of the person who caused it — one of their actors, a respect
ed professor who has not come
with the group.

The mock verdict is announced sentencing Miss Benare to kill
the child in her womb. The criminal act
with which she is charged becomes her punishment to be carried
out.
