Bharat - Vakya

Ganesh - Stuti

गजमुख ना निण पान्दव नेनेव
निज़वागि कस-त्यो-सो- वरगल
मूषिकनं वाहन मोदक हस्तनं
पाषंकुंशघर परम पवित्र

मोणेया तेरेयनु सरिसिदगें
निणेय दिणवनु भरिसिदगें
इं-धिन दिनदलि एल्लर कूणिसृत
मुदिन आटव ह्रडुवगें

वामम रुप महेश्वर पुत्र

मंगल-यर जाय मंगलम्

ब्बयामलकं वर्ण विलंबित सूत्र
Translation

He who parts the curtain on yestarday.
He who fills our tomarrow.
Translation
You with the elephant head, the son of Maheswara. You

who rides the mouse and carries a ’laddoo’ on his palm.

He who makes us dance today, and

He who designs the course of our future.
To him, Be praise and glory; praise and glory.

You who carries the santimonius ’Pashan—kushdhara’.

You in the ’Vaman—rupa’ and with ’Shyarnal-karna’.
Before you I prostrate to grant me a boon.

(The above stuties in Kannada are sung just before opening of the play and in end
of the play respectively.)
