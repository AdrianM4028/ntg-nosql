MUDRA RAKSHASA was born in a village near Lucknow in
1933 and graduated from the University of Lucknow in 1956,

MARJ'EEVA was the ﬁrst play he wrote. Since then he has
written three more — YOURS FAITHFULLY, TILCHA'I‘TA,
and TENDUA. His published works include three novels, a
collection of literary essays, a collection of short stories and
some poems. Up to his neck in trade union activities, he is
Chairman of The National Federation of A.I. R. Employees,

Vice-President of the Delhi Artistes’ Union and Adviser to The
Song and Drama Division Employees Association and Tarapore
Construction Workers Union.
