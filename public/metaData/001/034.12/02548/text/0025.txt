THE PLAY

GOLOK DHAM
(A play in three acts)
Adapted into Bengali by PROBAL GHOSH
(based on Friedrich Durenrnent and James Yaﬁe’s

Trapps/A Deadly Game)

On a stormy night when Abhimanyu Pratap has to take refuge in the solitery shelter housing
a group of enlightened retired men the condescends to participate in a past-time (game)
devised by his hosts.
Would you like to join when Abhimanyu parts? If you do then you must play it to the end.
