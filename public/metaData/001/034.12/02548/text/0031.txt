THE PLAY

ANUBIKSHAN
by C H A N D A N S E N

To the innocent onlooker the society scenes to be running on a smooth lines we almost
believe it as the reality of life.

But if we look through the microscope, if we really try to understand the world around us and
our individual position and role amidst the decaying degenerated world of ours—Then alone
we will ﬁnd a story, a story based in this reality ...............
And that is the real story of life. Our play ‘ANUBIKSHAN’ tries to unfold this story of
life. It is an attempt to cross the streets of metaphore, into the streets of reality.
