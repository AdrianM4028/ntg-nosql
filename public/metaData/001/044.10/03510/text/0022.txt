KAVALAM PADMANABHAN PANIKKAR

Atter training music under the tutelage of many eminent gurus at an early
age, he has been involved with theatre For several decades. He has been
an active member of the theatre group ” SOPANAM” under the guidence
of Shri. Kavalam Narayana Panikkar.

A rare combination of music composer and percussionist, he has
composed music for several Sanskrit plays and worked with various
directors like G.Aravindan in several Films and documentaries.
