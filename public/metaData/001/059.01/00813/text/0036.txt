Kala Mandir
Kala Mandir, Gwalior, was established in
1954. It has staged over 70 plays and
organized several theatre workshops
conducted by well known directors. The
group has also organized workshops on folk
theatre and publishes a theatre annual. Kala
Mandir established its repertory company in
1987.
