Revolving stage in action
“ Naatshala” has a revolving stage with multi dimensional features. Acceleration, retardation and revolving speed of the stage can
be programmed before any performance, widening the horizons of excellence in presentation. Enough number of sets can be
prepared according to requirements of different scenes in a play. The whole system is designed in such a way that when one scene
is being enacted the set for next scene can be changed without any hassle.
