'Asmita' stands committed to aesthetically innovative and socially relevant theatre.
It takes up contemporary issues to underline the contours of our time while
providing the best of entertainment. So far the group has staged Bhishma Sahni's
"Hanoosh" (26 shows), Albert Camus’ 'Caligula' (21 shows), Raghuvir
Chaudhary's 'Sikandra Sani (1 at Urdu Academy Festival-93), Dr. Girish Karnad's
"Tughlaq' (17 shows), 'Rakt-Kalyan' (37 shows), Dr. Dharamvir Bharti's

'Andhayug' (9 shows), Brecht's Exception & the Rule (street play) Shakespeare's
'Julius Caeser' (8 shows) & Merchant of Venice (as 'Dilfarosh' at Urdu
Academy's festival '96), Eugene C'Neil's Desire under the Elms (14 shows),
Swadesh Deepak’s Court-Martial (53 shows), Narendra Mohan's Qalander (Urdu
Academy's Festival 1997), Dario Fo's Operation Three Star (28 shows) Mahesh
Dattani's Final Solutions (22 shows) and Ashok Lal's Ek Mamooli Aadmi (27
Shows) Swadesh Deepak's Sabse Udaas Kavita (12 shows) Shakespare’s
Julius Caeser restaged in 1998 (2 shows). Brecht's Kanpur Ki Aurat Bhali
Ramkali (14 Shows) & "Log Baag" a play by Neil Simon (9 shows), Nag Bodas
Amma Tujhe Salaam (7 shows). All under the Direction of Aravind Gaur.
In support of people’s struggle against victimisation, “Asmita” gave street-shows
of Safdar Hashmi’s Aurat (36 shows) and Chicago 1886 (78 shows), Sanjha
Jahan (21 shows) and Tasveer (17 shows).
As an umbrella organisation to healthy theatre activities, "Asmita" organised
workshops at different colleges in Delhi which led to the production of Brecht's
The Good Person of Setzuan at l.P. College, Bhisham Sahni's Madhavi at
Gargi College, Dario Fo's Accidental Death of an Anarchist at School of Planning
& Architecture and Arthur Miller’s Crucible at LP. College. The group organised
the two-and—half hour long solo performance of Piyush Mishra which enthralled
the audience at Jaipur and Calcutta also.

In recognition of its endeavours, the group has been invited to perform Court
Martial as the only entry in Hindi at National Drama Festival at Calicut (Kerala),
'Asmita' has also performed 'Court Martial‘ at Dibrugarh (Chayanika), Assam
and Jammu & Kashmir, 'Final Solutions' in Jabalpur and Gwalior 'Dperation
Three Star‘ in Begu Sarai (IPTA Golden Jubilee Festival), Shakespeare's "Julius

Caesar' at Dibrugarh and Sibsagar (Assam) for the National Shakespeare Festival

organised by Chayanika, scenes from Julius Caesar, Sabse Udaas Kavita and
Rakt Kalyan at the Prithvi International Theatre Festival (November 1998).
Also performed Rakt Kalyan on the occasion of Girish Karnad being bestowed
the Kalidaas Samman and Gyanpeeth Award. Also staged "Ek Mamooli Aadmi"
for Bhartiya Rang Mahotsava (1999).
