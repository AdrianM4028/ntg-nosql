Gautam Majumdar
३

After having completed his graduation in Dramatic Arts from
Rabindra Bharti University in Acting in 1992, he completed his
postgraduate Diploma in Design and Direction From the National
School ofDrama in I 99 7.
He has worked as a Mime & Movement instructor at the Jogesh
Mime Academy from 1993 to 1994. He is presently also a regular
mimejudge at the All India University Association. A t present he is a

visitingfaculty at the National School ofDrama. He has co-ordinated
various theatre workshops in the country and has collaborated with
innumerable Indian andforeign directors in the capacity ofdesigner;
for which he has travelled extensively to countries like Finland,
Moscow, Denmark, Germany, England, Japan, South Africa and
Shrilanka. His areas ofspecialization in design are Light Design and
Set- property Design and execution.
