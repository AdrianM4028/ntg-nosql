MUSIC
Vidya Rao

A singer of thumri - dadra, Vidya Rao trained under Prof B. N.
Datta and Smt. Naina Devi, and is currently learning from Pandit
Mani Prasad and Smt. Shanti Hiranand. She has performed
extensively in India and abroad. She has also collaborated with other
musicians, dancers, writers and theatre persons to create special

performances. She has designed music, composed and sungforﬁlms
and the theatre. Her work includes the acclaimed plays Umrao,
Sundari.‘ An Actor Prepares and Navlakkha. She has researched and
written on music with the help ofgrants from the Ford Foundation,
and the Department ofCulture. She has taught a semester as visiting
professor at JNU’s school ofArts and Aesthetics and has conducted
several workshops in music including at the National School of
Drama.
