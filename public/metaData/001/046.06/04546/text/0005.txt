Director

KATHRIN SIEVERS
Kathrin Sievers : Born in 1966, in North of Germany. After finishing high School, She went to
University to study German literature and chinese. She spent two years in Taiwan and China study
in and working and completed her M.A. Meanwhile She decided to be a stage-director and had
already got introduced in theatre work in Germany. After exams in 1994 she went to Vienna/Austria
to work in one of the most famous repertory companies in Europe as a drarnaturg, but left that after

some time to become a stage director's assistant in company back in Germany. After 3 years of
training, She started her free lance career as director. Since then she has done around 18 play, including comedies, serious plays, most
of them contemporary ones.
