When poetry is transported to the stage it changes in many ways.
What we are presenting here is Muktiboth's 'Andhere Mein',
but it is also 'Andhere Mein' of Suman Kumar, actors & artists
of Punashcha There are many dangers in staging a poetry that
is meant to be read But Punashcha has been trying in the last
two years to explore the possibilities of staging poetry without
killing its soul. We have made similar experiments with 'Aurat
Ka Desh' based on a collection of poems by Raghuveer Sahay,
'Syah Samandar Ke Pakhi' based on the autobiographical
narration of Dalit writers, 'Main Philosopher Nahin' based on
another poem by Muktibodh and 'Magadh' based on the poems
by Srikant Verrna. 'Andhere Mein' is another modest attempt in
the same direction
