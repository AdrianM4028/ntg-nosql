Born on 5th February, 4948, Krishna Mathur learnt music
from Guru Sohil Punjabi, Chunni Kale and Giriraj Ji. At the
young age of 16 years she got involved with theatre by
taking part in Nautanki and Musicals of Brij area.
At the occasion of 25th anniversary celebration of Kaka
Hathrasi's poetry, she enacted the role of Taramati in the
play Satya Harishchandra. For the benefit of rural schools
of Mathura distt., She collected money by performing
Nautanki shows. She has given her performance at Uttar
Pradesh, Haryana, Rajasthan & Kolkata etc. For the past
30 years she is associated with the All India Radio and
also presented two Swang (Nautanki) on Doordarshan.
