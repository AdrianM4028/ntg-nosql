SANJEEV BAKSHI

(b.1967) A graduate in

Commerce started his career in theatre as an

actor. He joined Natrang in 1985 and went on to
perform in several plays - Bawa Jitto, Mahabhoj,

Poster, Kasai Barah and Kisi Ek Phool Ka Naam
Lo - being some of the popular ones. He has

participated in plays not only at state level but also
at national level. Among the various awards to his
credit is the distinction .of~ being adjudged Best

Actor in All India Cultural Competition in 1987
.Recently he has earned the distinction of being
selected as a professional stage actor by Sri
Ram Centre for Art & Culture, New Delhi.
With “Aadhe Adhure” this versatile young
man makes his debut as a director.
