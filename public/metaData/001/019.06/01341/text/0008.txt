VIDYA BHUSHAN (b. 4904) is a graduate in vocal
music. His debut in theatre came in 1980 with Dogra
Mandal production ' Pacheesvin Shatabdi'. He later

joined NATRANG and performed in plays like
'Chauraha',‘ Ek Sanghrash Ka Ant', Yeh Laash Kiski

Hai' and 'Kaun Nachega Naach'. He was also featured
prominently in 'Mahabhoj' and 'Bawa Jitto' which were
presented at national level. He holds the distinction of
being an approved artist of Doordarshan Kendra,
Jalandhar.
In ‘Adhe Adhure" he will be seen as Ashok.
