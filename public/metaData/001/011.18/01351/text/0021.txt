The Director - PROBIR GUHA
Probir Guha, playwright-director born May 5, 1947, has
directed all the productions under the banner of TLL. He has
acted and directed plays since 1965. After producing several .
plays for street theater and political propaganda for and with
other groups, he formed the Living Theater in 1977 and then
Theatre Living Laboratory in 1984, performing in intimate environment spaces, often picking up local and regional themes and experiences for immediate presentation and critical analysis. He is
trying to improvise a production performance method to communi-

cate with rurourban audiences sharply divided by dialected and
linguistic variations. He is searching for a method that would draw

upon folk idioms from different parts of the country and is trying
to discover approaches, methods and psychological devices to
organize these experiences into a single idiom.
