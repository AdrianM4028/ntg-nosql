Nissar Allana
Nissar Allana was born on 12th August,
1946 in Mumbai. At present he is the
Director of "The Dramatic Art and
Design Academy" (DADA) a training
Institute in acting and Design and
"Theatre and television associates' a
production company. He is also a
Scenographer, Director, Lighting
Designer for theatre and television.
He took his theatre training at the
Schaubuhne Am Halleschen Ufer, Berlin
in 1974 and also worked with Karl-Ernst

Hermann and Peter Stein as a Set Designer.
He has designed over 58 theatre productions for stage
