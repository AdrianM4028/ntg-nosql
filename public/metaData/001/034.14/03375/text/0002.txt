about the play

“Banailey” is a play apposite to the

contemporary times, a time when consumerism has
taken over and genuine human ethos have taken a
beating. In this play Man’s perpetual greed has been
contrasted against his anabolic tendencies for
harmony and wisdom.
about the director

Suman Kumar, a graduate from
National School of Drama, passed the Certiﬁcate

Course in Design from Mountview Theatre School,
London.

He was awarded Charles Wallace India Trust
Scholarship in 1998 and fellowship from NSD in
2000. He has also participated in the International
Theatre Festival at Copenhagen. He has been
associated with ‘Bahroop’ as an NSD expert under
extension teaching programme.
about the group
Bahroop came into existence in 1996,

when a group of people from different walks of life
decided to share its experience with a larger section

of the society. Its productions Kale Kazm'e Ne Kaha.
Dekh Tamas/2a Dekh. Baaghica Bachharam Ka and
Zaharqcmd were awarded by Delhi Urdu Academy
in 1997, 1999, 2000 and 2001 respectively.

Other successful productions are Kath Jogi.
.Madhvz'. Tootte Parivesh and Chand Zamz'n Ka

Tukra A ur Mein. Bahroop has also organized talks.
seminars and workshops with eminent personalities
like Habib Tanvir, J.N. Kaushal, Vinit Kumar Sinha.

Mushtak Kak, Chittaranjan Tripathi. Shrikant
Kishore, Suman Kumar, Piyush Mishra. Kamal
Kishore, Arvind Gaur, JD. Ashwathama, Suresh

Bharadwaj, Satyabarat Raut and Surendra Sharma.
Bahroop also publishes a theatre magazine.
