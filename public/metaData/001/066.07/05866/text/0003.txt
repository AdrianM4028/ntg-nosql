Evolving from researches
workshops and rehearsals
since 1992, Ramkatha
Ramkahani incorporates
the essence of the Indian

story telling traditions and
the sensibilities of classical
kathak. It has drawn

inspiration from myriad
sources. It is a total theatre
and contains drama dance

music and poetry to create
a receptive audience
which can receive and

enjoy the essence of the
Ramkatha and can find

peace and solace in the
love admiration and
devotion which Ram and

Ramkatha generate.
