MEMBERSHIP OF THE LITTLE THEATRE G-ROUP
Membership is open to all those interested in dramatic art and craft.

classes of members.

There are four

Life Members, Active Members, Theatre Members and Associate Members.

Life Members are entitled to all the advantages of Active Members, for life.

Active Members are those who associate themselves with the Group in an active way.
They are required to work and collaborate regularly in the presentation and production of plays
and other activities. They are entitled to all advantages enjoyed by Theatre Members.
Theatre Members are those who associate themselves with the Group in a general way.

They are entitled to receive one free ticket at each L.T.G. show, concessions on other tickets as
and when the Committee so decide, priority in booking, free copy of the Group Bulletin and
free admission to Study Circle meetings and lectures.
Associate Members are those members who are on the rolls of educational institutions

and under the age of 20 years.

They are entitled to one free ticket at each L.T.G. show.

The Little Theatre Group is a registered non-proﬁt-rnaking, non-political society, its
activities being conﬁned to the art of the drama and theatre. It is recognised by the Sangeet

Natak Akaderni. For full details please write to The Hon. General Secretary, The Little Theatre
Group, 10, Shankar Market, New Delhi.
Membership is subject to the approval of the Executive Committee.
