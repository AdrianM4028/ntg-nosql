The propitiation generally entails goat-sacriﬁces or sacriﬁce of some other animal.
Finally, the spirits are sent back to their abodes by the Das after their blessings have been obtained. Gradually the spell created by his singing breaks and
the ceremony comes to . a close. In the state of trance as the dancers can lick redhot iron bars or shove their hands into the blazing ﬁre without coming to harm.
The instruments used are Dholeagara, Hurka and Thali (a bronze plate).
