THE DIRECTOR

Devendra Rai Ankur
MA. in Hindi literature from University of Delhi and
holder of Diploma from National School of Drama with
specialization in Direction.

An important name in Modern Indian Theatre among
young theatre directors. Pioneered a new genre in Indian

Theatre namely ’Kahani Ka Rangmanch’ with the
production of ’Teen Ekant' (Three tests in solitude) with
National School of Drama Repertory Company in 1975.
Have directed 85 stories, 12 novels and 30 plays with
different amateur and professional repertories all over
India during the last seventeen years and held several
theatre workshops.
Also worked in Bengali, Oriya, Kannada and Kurnayuni
apart from Hindi and _Urdu. Founder member and a
leading director of ’Sambhav'- a famous theatre group of
Delhi.
His other famous productions are ’Mahanagar-Teen
Samvad’ ’Mahabhoj’ ’Dar Se Bichhudi’ ’Godan’ ’Anaro'
’Satya Harishchandra’ ’Agra Bazaar’ ’Urubhang' ’Andher
Nagri' ’Premchand Ka Gaon' ’Sarnskara’ ’Chhe Bigha
Zameen' and ’Lautana: Baar-Baar'.

At present Associate Professor for Theatre Architecture at
National School of Drama. Also taught Production Process
and Modern Indian Drama.
