PRODUCTIONS & PERFORMANCES
——

Nandikar, in its 26 years, has flourished,
weakened, split, revived and continued
to produce plays of unknown Bengali
authors and Rabindranath Thakur and of
both classical and modern masters like
Sophocles, Euripides, Visakhadatta,
Ibsen, Strindberg, Chekhov, Pirandello,
O’neill, Ionesco, Anouilh, Wesker,
Terson, Kurosawa, Bergman & Brecht

from all over the world
