Sayed Jamil Ahmed
1Sayed Jamil Ahmed is a professor at the Department of
Theatre and Music, University of Dhaka. He graduated from
National School of Drama, New Delhi in 4978 and obtained his
MA in Theatre Studies from the University of Warwick (UK) and
completed his Ph D from the University of Dhaka. He founded
the Department of Theatre and Music at the University of
Dhaka and severed as its first Chair till1997. He has to his
credit over 50 research essays and has directed over 20 plays
including the prestigious The Wheel, Yellow Springs etc.He
has also designed the sets and the lights for over 70
performances in Bangladesh and India. He is a Fulbright fellow
and also recipient of several awards including the Munir
chowduryAwardand Nandikar NationaITtheatre Award.
He received two Fulbright fellowships (1990, 2005), has published essays in RIDE, ATJ,
TDR, and NTO, and has taught at Antioch College, USA (1990), King Alfred's College,
UK (2002), and San Francisco City College, USA (2005). Books published in English are
Acinpakhi Infinity: Indigenous Theatre in Bangladesh ( U n ive rsity Press Limited, 2000)
and In Praise of Niranjan: Islam, Theatre, and Bangladesh (Pathak Sarnabesh , 2003) .
