Salim al DeenIS a leading playwright of Bangladesh. His dramatic work has been seen
on stage, film, and televisionIn Bangladesh, India, Britain and the United States. HeIs

professor of drama at Jahanginar University.
Salim Al -Deen holds BA. degree with honours in Bengali (1970) and MA. in the same
subject (1973) from the university of Dhaka. His major achievements as a playwright
include 'Kittan Khola', 'KeramatMangal', 'Hat Hadai', 'Chaka' and 'Jaibati Kanyar Mon'. A

translation of Chaka has also been directed by Steve Friedman in New York( 1 994) . From
Kittan Khola onwards, AI-Deen has attempted to incorporate indigenous theatrical
elements(including the narrative form)in his plays and evolve a language capable of
reflecting the traditional heritage of Bangladesh, imbibed with contemporary relevance.
A poet in the truest sense, Al -Deen’s plays are literally woven with gossamer which
capture the essence with briefest strokes and subverts the reality for revealing the
fathornless depths which lie beyond.
