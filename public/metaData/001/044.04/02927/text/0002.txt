The Play

He is called the main character. He Psycologically rebels against his over-protective
parents, and plans a tour ofthe world on his bicycle. During this tour which takes place
in his Fantasy, he encounters various characters of Life and Imagination.
The Voyage is a surfacing of his inner supressed frustrations and dreams. The
culmination of this play is tragic, not in the Grecian sense, but as a reﬂection of the
decedence of our values and attitudes.
