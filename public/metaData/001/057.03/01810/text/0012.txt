The Director

plays he has directed. Worked as Assistant Director of
Bharat Bhavan's Rangrnandal and as Visiting Professor

A recipient of Senior Fellowship from the Department of
Culture, Ministry of Human Resources, Alakhnandan is
now working with his troupe NATA BUNDELE. Has
worked with renowned directors like B.V. Karanth, K. N.
Panicker, Badal Sircar, Peter Brook, Fritz Bennewits,
