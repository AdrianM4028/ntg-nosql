With a Masters in English literature from
Delhi University and a Diploma in
Drarnatics with specialization in Direction from
the National School of Drama, Kirti Jain has

acted in and directed several plays with theatre
groups both in and outside NSD. She has
conducted theatre workshops in several cities in
India and abroad; has contributed articles in
Hindi and English to theatre related books and
journals, including entries in The Oxford
Companion to Theatre; and is a member of the
Editorial Board of the theatre journal, Studies in Theatre and

Performance, published by the Exeter University, UK. She has
produced plays and documentaries for Doordarshan; and is
Trustee of the Natarang Pratishthan, a theatre resource
centre, under whose auspices she organized Poorva — An

Asian Women Directors‘ Festival and Conference, and an
Asian Workshop on Documentation of Theatre as Project
Director. Under the auspices of the Natarang Pratishthan she
has also documented performances and work process of
several Indian theatre directors. She joined the NSD as
Faculty in 1977 and went on to become the School‘s Director
from 198831995. During her directorship Prof. Jain started
the TIE Company, the documentation and publication
programmers and the Regional Resource Centre of NSD at
Bangalore.
