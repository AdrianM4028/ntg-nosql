The Director
Suresh Anagalli born in l 961 in Anagalli, a coastal village in Karnataka, started his theatre journey
at a very young age. In his college days too, he was associated with Samudaya, a progressive
theater movement. He later joined the Ninasarn Theatre lnstitute, Heggodu, and then graduated

from the NSD in 1985, with a specialization in direction. He has worked for the Ranga Mandala
Repertory, Bhopal and is now headingthe NSD Regional Centre, Bangalore, for two years. He has
visited Japan and Sri Lanka under the cultural exchange programme of the Government of India. He
has been a recipient of Karnataka Nataka Akademy Award (1998) for his contribution to Kannada
theatre.
As a director, Suresh has over 60 productions to his credit, including Jujbi Devara Jugai Aata (Dario
Fo's Archangels Don't Play Pinballs), H S Shivaprakash's Manteswamy Katha Prasanga(which has
completed a record number of nearly 300 shows, rare in amateur theatre) Chandrashekhar
Kambar's Mahamayi, Moliere‘s Scoundrel Scapin, Edward Bond's Narrow Road To The Deep North,
to cite a few.
