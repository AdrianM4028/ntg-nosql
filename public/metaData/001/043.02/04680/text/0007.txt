Mahakavi Bhas
It was not until 1912 that one got to know about the work of Bhasa. It was under the editorship of T.Ganapati
Shastri , that the ﬁrst series of thirteen plays discovered were attributed to the poet. Till then what we knew of
Bhasa was simply his high reputation, which surfaced from the work of his successors. Kalidasa in his ﬁrst work,
Malavikagnimitram , refers to Bhasa with Saumilla, Kaviputra and others as his great predecessors in the art,
whose fame renders difficult the acceptance of the work of an untried author.

It is however, difﬁcult to arrive at any precise determination of Bhasa’s date. " The opinions of scholars may be
divided under two broad spectra within which Bhasa's date ﬂuctuates. This broad divergence is itself due to the
two different trends of opinion still current, regarding the date of Kalidasa , one placing him in the 1 st century B .C.
along with the traditional Vikramaditya of Ujjain and the other in the golden age of the Guptas with Chandragupta
the II who ruled from 315 to 414 AD. and also assumed to the title of Vikramaditya."'l‘hose who support the
Gupta age for Kalidasa place Bhasa in the 2nd or 3rd century AD. The other group place him as early as the 5th
century B .C.

The dominating inﬂuence in Bhasa‘s style, was clearly that of the epic writer Valmiki, whose work inevitably

impressed itself on the minds of all his successors. The effects are visible not merely in the dramas with epic
subject matter, but extended throughout Bhasa's plays.
