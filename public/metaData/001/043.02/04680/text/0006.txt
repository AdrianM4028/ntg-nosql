About The Play
Bhas Ghatotkach is a unified production with Bhas's three plays - Madhyarn Vyayog, Duta Vakyam and Duta
Ghatotkacha. Bhas has concieved instances from the epic Mahabharatha to create these plays. These are plays
which have relevance in the present context of the world too. This play reads a graph that begins and concludes
with the character of Ghatotkach. He is the son of Hidimba and Bhirn. There is a reunion of this family in this
play. At the same time, there is yet another family, the Bhramin family. The various shades of every member of
the family, makes space for the variety in the play. Madhyarn Vyayog emphasizes on the intricacies of the family
— as a unit.
In parallel , Ghatotkach belongs to the Kaurava dynasty also just as he belongs to his mother's tribe. He is
involved into the Mahabharat war too at a later stage. Dutavakya is the play which strings Madhyarnvyayog and
DutaGhatotkach. In Dutavakya, Krishna comes in as a messenger to convey to Duryodhana the requisition of
the Pandavas which if accepted could have led to a cease of the disharmony. This does not happen and hence the
catastrophic war happens.
Ghatotkach comes in as a messenger of the Pandavas in DutaGhatotkach. A few sentences spoken by Ghatotkach
highlights the sensibilities of himself and his mother's tribe(Rakshas) which seem to be more humane than the
residents of the palace.
There is an inclination in this play to channelise focus on the contemporary situations around us in the society
where the family, politics, society and peace have a strange entangling.
