Born in Adhar Sattra, Dergaon, Assam in the year 1933. Guru started his
initial lessons from his father,Dharanidhar Goswami.He had received

further training in Sattiya from Gopiram Bayan, Babula Bayan of Adhar
Sattra. He has founded ’Alok Shilpi Sangha’,
a centre for dance and music in Dergaon. He has carried on an extensive
research on the theoretical and practical aspects of Indian dances under

the guidance of Kalaguru Bishnu Prasad Rabha. He had also under gone
training for Kathak under the tutelage of guru Ganesh Hiralal at Jaipur.

In the year 1956 he earned accolades from the Maharani of Jaipur for his
performance of Kathak at the royal court. He has also received training
