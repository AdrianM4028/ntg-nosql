“Khalid Ki Khala ” is a farcical play written by Qudsia Zaidi in the year 1954 based on the original Charley’s Aunt
which was written 100 years ago.
The purpose of selecting this play for the NSD Repertory Company is that it has many characters that
I have found suitable for the repertory artists, as it gives all of them an equal opportunity to perform!

Also it provides the artists an opportunity to be fluent in the Urdu language, and gives us an idea of whether
such a revisiting of a classical comedy play works today or not.

I am pleased to note that the artists of the Repertory Company have been quite enthusiastic in their response
to this play.

। thank Prof. Waman Kendre, Director, National School of Drama and Sh. Suresh Sharrna, Chief of Repertory
Company, for inviting me to do this play with the School's repertory artists. | wish all these talented artists
a bright future.
